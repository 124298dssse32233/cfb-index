The Auburn Tonal-Shift Prediction

In December we said Auburn's offseason would be quieter than expected. The April gate confirms it.

By The Receipts Desk 3 MIN READ VOL. I · NO. 14

On December 18, 2025, we predicted that Auburn's January–April conversation volume would be the quietest of any post-bowl SEC fanbase. The cohort velocity index, as of April 1, has Auburn fourth-quietest among SEC programs.

Aged well, with a footnote: Vanderbilt finished even quieter.
