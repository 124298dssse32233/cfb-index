Chronicle

Two seasons that defined the modern frame

2013

The First Golden Window closes

James Franklin’s third year. Three straight bowl appearances. First top-25 finish since 1948. Franklin leaves for Penn State the following January. The program would not finish ranked again in the next decade.

2024

Three wins in the SEC is real proof of life

Clark Lea’s fourth year. Beat Alabama in Nashville for the first time since 1984. Beat South Carolina and Auburn in the same month. The League called the wins a scheduling accident. The program called them a standard.
