Tier strategy — why Alabama and Vandy in this set

From IMPLEMENTATION_PLAN_v3 Part C. The 17 profiled teams split into three tiers calibrated by traffic × cultural relevance. The mockup set deliberately shows one Tier-S program (Alabama) and one Tier-B program (Vanderbilt) to prove the Profile archetype scales without bespoke renderers per team.

TIER S · Hand-tailored editorial

5 teams

Alabama · Ohio State · Michigan · Notre Dame · Texas — estimated 60%+ of team-page traffic. Each gets: full bespoke renderer per v5-9, custom IA, 5+ rituals with custom SVGs, Pattern D adversarial-critique editorial on tentpole weeks, custom hero typography, featured in tentpole rotation. ~3-5 sessions per team after foundations ship.

Represented in this set: mockup_02 Alabama

TIER A · Programmatic bespoke

8 teams

Auburn · Florida · Georgia · Oklahoma · Oregon · Penn State · Tennessee · USC. Shared bespoke renderer with per-team voice palette, 3-5 rituals with ligne-claire SVGs, Pattern C standard editorial, shared hero typography, standard tentpole treatment. ~1-2 sessions per team after Tier S template proven.

Represented in this set: not yet — Tier A renders use the same Profile archetype as Alabama with different voice + ritual cards

TIER B · Profile-driven distinct

4 teams

Massachusetts · UConn · Vanderbilt · Washington. Shared renderer with profile-frontmatter customization, 3 rituals each, Pattern C standard editorial, honest framing of each team's actual product context. ~0.5-1 session per team. Tier promotion is one-way and triggered by 3-of-4: (1) traffic above Tier-S median for 4 consecutive weeks, (2) CFP playoff field, (3) 5★ commit / NFL pick within 30 days, (4) 3+ tentpole edition cover essays.

Represented in this set: mockup_03 Vanderbilt — shows the same archetype with a top-rung "Beat the rival" ladder instead of "National Championship"

Effort math: 5×3 + 8×1.5 + 4×0.75 = 30 sessions for all 17 profiled teams (vs naive 17×3 = 51). Saves ~21 sessions / ~5 weeks across the v5-9 sprint.
