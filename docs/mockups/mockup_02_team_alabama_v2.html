Chronicle

Two echoes from the program’s era ledger

2009

Title #1 of The Process

Saban’s third year. The defense gave up 11.7 points per game. Mark Ingram became Alabama’s first Heisman. The frame for everything the program would become in the next fifteen years was set on a January night in Pasadena.

2024

First miss since 2007

DeBoer’s first year. 9–4, no SEC title game, no CFP. The first time since the season before Saban arrived that the program finished outside the national title conversation. The 2026 spring read is whether the infrastructure compounds or regresses.
