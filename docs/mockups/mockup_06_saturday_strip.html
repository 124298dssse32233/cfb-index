Reading guide

How to read this mockup

Close
Mobile-only primitive Spec H.1. 44px tall live state, 56px expanded. Sticks to top of viewport on mobile only — desktop has the full site nav instead. Phone frames here are at 390px (iPhone 15 Pro width).
In-season vs off-season Two render states from the same component. In-season shows live games with pulsing LIVE dot; off-season shows kickoff countdown + portal commits + camp markers. The single CFBD call swap is the only logic change.
prefers-reduced-motion The pulsing LIVE dot animation is paused under reduced-motion. The horizontal-scroll snap-points are also honored as instant rather than animated.
Bottom navigation (v5-7.6) 5-item bottom nav: Hub / Daily / Heisman / Teams / Search. Touch targets 44×44 min. Search is the Command-K affordance for mobile (v5-11.5).
Performance budget Strip payload <5KB. Single CFBD call cached 30s during games, 5 min upcoming, 1 hour off-season. Strip render <500ms FCP target on 4G.
iOS chrome detail Dynamic-island-shaped notch and home indicator included for visual fidelity. Not part of the actual render — just makes the mockup feel like a real phone in review.
