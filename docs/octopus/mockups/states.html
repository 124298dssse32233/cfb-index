05 Empty & low-data states

Never a blank, a zero, or “N/A”. Each is a specific sentence that says why and what would change it — quoted verbatim from the microcopy guide §5.3. This is the accountability thesis made visible: we’d rather show nothing than fake a reading.

06 Error states

Factual, never “Oops!” or an error code. Say what broke and give the one button that fixes it. The message is role="alert". Stale-data is informational (amber), not alarming — a late deploy still shows the last good board.

! The board didn’t load. A hiccup fetching this week’s ratings.
! This chart didn’t load. The numbers are still available as a table below.  
⟳ Showing the last good board from 9:05 PM. Tonight’s update is still publishing — this refreshes automatically.

The rules every state obeys

  • Color is never the only signal. Every belief chip, confidence band, delta, and winner-highlight pairs color with a word or glyph — AA, and colorblind-safe.
  • Focus-visible is sacred. A 2px navy ring on every focusable control. Outlines are never removed without a replacement.
  • Touch targets ≥ 48px hit-area even when the glyph is small. Hover effects sit behind @media (hover:hover) so phones don’t get stuck-hover.
  • Reduced motion kills transforms, the roll-up numerals, and the spinner — opacity and instant states remain. The skeleton→content swap is layout, not animation.
  • Loading announces. The region carries aria-busy + a label; errors are role="alert"; result counts and re-sorts fire aria-live="polite".
  • Empty ≠ broken. “Awaiting signal” is a designed state with calm copy — never a zero, a blank, or an “N/A”.
