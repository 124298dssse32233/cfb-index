METHOD · Weighted log-odds with informative Dirichlet prior (Monroe · Colaresi · Quinn, "Fightin' Words"); z ≥ 1.96 + minimum-count floor on every term. Lollipop dots sit on a log10 axis anchored at 1× (parity); the printed multiplier is the source of truth — geometry only dramatizes it. Word-wall and waterfall type weight is set by the data (variable fonts: Hepta Slab, weight 100–900). Corpus: 62,690 posts / 3.74M words after excluding podcasts & news wires (ad-reads and bylines aren't fan voice), city subreddits (residents aren't fans), and posts failing a football-relevance gate. Mirror rates normalized per 1,000 words of each side's own corpus, equal scales. Personality: percentile ranks over per-mention sentiment / emotion / sarcasm (CardiffNLP encoder stack), 17-fanbase cohort, median tick at 50.
