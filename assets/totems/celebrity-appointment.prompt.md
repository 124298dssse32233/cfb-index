# Celebrity Appointment — Archetype Totem 15

## One-line brief
Two red-carpet stanchion posts connected by an amber velvet rope. Visual shorthand for the program that hired a name, not a coach.

## Visual family
Ligne-claire totem, warm-ink + amber on bone paper. Matches locked master plate.

## Full prompt used
```
Single standalone 1:1 square illustration — not a grid, not a contact sheet, not panels. One image only.

Match the uploaded totem_master.png style exactly: flat ligne-claire on bone paper #F3EEE4, uniform 2px warm-ink contour, flat fills only, no hatching, no shadow, no gradients. Clean mid-century European cartooning discipline.

SUBJECT: two red-carpet stanchion posts connected by a single draped velvet rope. The posts have round bases and finial tops. The rope sags slightly in a shallow catenary curve between them. No humans, no logos, no carpet.

AMBER: the rope is amber #E0A300. The stanchions are warm ink. Exactly one amber detail.

COMPOSITION: the two-stanchion unit off-center right, negative space at left, ~20% paper margin, eye-level view.
```

## Reference images
- `assets/masters/totem_master.png`

## Model + settings
- ChatGPT Pro, Art Director Custom GPT (gpt-image-1 backend), 1:1, single image.

## Selection rationale
Minimalist and legible — two stanchions with amber catenary rope between them, nothing else. The rope curves in a clean shallow U, not a hard hang, which reads as "presentation rope, not functional barrier." Composition drifted to dead-center (vs. master's off-center-right rule). Accepted because two-object symmetry here serves the metaphor (two posts flanking nothing = velvet-rope theater with no actual gatekeeping). Post finials tipped in amber as well — minor creep, in-system.

## Rejection notes
None — single-image workflow, committed on first generation.
