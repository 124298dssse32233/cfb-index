# Hopeful Uprising — Archetype Totem 04

## One-line brief
A sapling with an amber bud pushing up through a fractured concrete bleacher. Visual shorthand for the program finding green shoots through bad architecture.

## Visual family
Ligne-claire totem, warm-ink + amber on bone paper. Matches locked master plate (Anxious Dynasty).

## Full prompt used
```
Single standalone 1:1 square illustration — not a grid, not a contact sheet, not panels. One image only.

Match the uploaded totem_master.png style exactly: flat ligne-claire on bone paper #F3EEE4, uniform 2px warm-ink #0B0F14 contour, flat fills only, no hatching, no shadow, no gradients. Clean mid-century European cartooning discipline.

SUBJECT: a young green sapling growing up through a jagged crack in a slab of concrete bleacher seating. Two small leaves. Roots just visible at the crack edge. No humans, no logos.

AMBER: a single small bud on the sapling's topmost stem is amber #E0A300. Exactly one amber detail.

COMPOSITION: object off-center right, negative space at left, ~20% paper margin.
```

- No curation — first keep.

## Selection rationale
Clean three-element read: cracked concrete (the thing being pushed through), green sapling (the thing pushing), amber bud (the accent). The amber bud lands at the upper tip of the plant — the eye naturally travels root → leaves → bud, which is the metaphor's internal motion. Composition places the stepped bleacher diagonal at lower-right with the plant rising into the upper-right quadrant, preserving left-side negative space per master plate.

## Rejection notes
None — single-image workflow, committed on first generation.
