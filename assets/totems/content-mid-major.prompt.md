# Content Mid-Major — Archetype Totem 07

## One-line brief
A trifold pocket schedule with every game date neatly circled — one in amber. Visual shorthand for the program that's just happy to be playing.

## Visual family
Ligne-claire totem, warm-ink + amber on bone paper. Matches locked master plate (Anxious Dynasty).

## Full prompt used
```
Single standalone 1:1 square illustration — not a grid, not a contact sheet, not panels. One image only.

Match the uploaded totem_master.png style exactly: flat ligne-claire on bone paper #F3EEE4, uniform 2px warm-ink contour, flat fills only, no hatching, no shadow, no gradients. Clean mid-century European cartooning discipline.

SUBJECT: a small trifold pocket schedule card opened flat, showing a grid of twelve game dates. Every single date is neatly circled in warm ink with a small hand-drawn ring. No team names visible, no logos — just the grid and the circles.

AMBER: one of the circles — pick the middle one — is amber #E0A300 instead of warm ink. Exactly one amber detail.

COMPOSITION: schedule card off-center right, negative space at left, ~20% paper margin.
```

## Reference images

- No curation — first keep.

## Selection rationale
Composition is centered rather than off-center right (drift from master plate's hard rule). Accepted because the content of the schedule — each date in its own circle — reads as satisfied ritual more than as anxious ambition, which is exactly what the archetype demands. The amber-ringed SEP 27 ("the game circled hardest on every Content Mid-Major's calendar") is a single clean accent that anchors the eye without overpowering. Centered composition noted as acceptable variant for grid-heavy subjects where radial symmetry serves the metaphor.

## Rejection notes
None — single-image workflow, committed on first generation.
