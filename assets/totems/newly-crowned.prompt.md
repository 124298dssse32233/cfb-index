# Newly Crowned — Archetype Totem 09

## One-line brief
A champion's belt inside its open presentation box with the hangtag still attached. Visual shorthand for the program that just won and hasn't yet learned the cost.

## Visual family
Ligne-claire totem, warm-ink + amber on bone paper. Matches locked master plate.

## Full prompt used
```
Single standalone 1:1 square illustration — not a grid, not a contact sheet, not panels. One image only.

Match the uploaded totem_master.png style exactly: flat ligne-claire on bone paper #F3EEE4, uniform 2px warm-ink contour, flat fills only, no hatching, no shadow, no gradients. Clean mid-century European cartooning discipline.

SUBJECT: a generic champion's belt resting inside an open rectangular presentation box. The belt has a circular center plate, no specific logo or wording. A small paper hangtag is still attached by a string to the belt. The box lid is open upward. No humans, no real team logos.

AMBER: the belt's circular center plate is amber #E0A300. Exactly one amber detail.

COMPOSITION: box off-center right, negative space at left, ~20% paper margin, 3/4 front view.
```

## Reference images
- `assets/masters/totem_master.png`

## Model + settings
- ChatGPT Pro, Art Director Custom GPT (gpt-image-1 backend), 1:1, single image.

## Selection rationale
The open box reads cleanly from 3/4 front, the belt's strap-with-center-plate silhouette is unambiguous, and the hangtag on a visible string is the detail that does the emotional work — the belt is so new it still has price-tag energy. Amber center plate dominates correctly. Two small amber side-plates came through as well (minor amber creep beyond the "exactly one" rule); accepted because they form a single visual unit with the center plate rather than reading as separate accents.

## Rejection notes
None — single-image workflow, committed on first generation.
