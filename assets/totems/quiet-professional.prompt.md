# Quiet Professional — Archetype Totem 05

## One-line brief
A wooden clipboard holding a neat X-and-O play diagram with a coach's whistle resting on its lanyard. Visual shorthand for the program that just goes to work.

## Visual family
Ligne-claire totem, warm-ink + amber on bone paper. Matches locked master plate (Anxious Dynasty).

## Full prompt used
```
Single standalone 1:1 square illustration — not a grid, not a contact sheet, not panels. One image only.

Match the uploaded totem_master.png style exactly: flat ligne-claire on bone paper #F3EEE4, uniform 2px warm-ink contour, flat fills only, no hatching, no shadow, no gradients. Clean mid-century European cartooning discipline.

SUBJECT: a wooden clipboard lying flat, holding a single sheet with a neat X-and-O play diagram in warm ink. A clean metal coach's whistle rests beside it on a lanyard. No humans, no team logos, no text beyond the Xs and Os.

AMBER: the whistle body is amber #E0A300. Exactly one amber detail.

COMPOSITION: object grouping off-center right, negative space at left, ~20% paper margin.
```

## Selection rationale
The play diagram reads cleanly — X defenders above, O offensive alignment below, two arrows indicating routes, one dashed indicating motion. The whistle's amber body is the singular accent. Wooden clipboard substrate reads as a neutral third color within the master-plate's implicit "substrate neutrals are fine" allowance (same pattern as the wooden plank in Perpetual Believer). Lanyard falls off the right edge which adds life.

None — single-image workflow, committed on first generation.
