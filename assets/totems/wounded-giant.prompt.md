# Wounded Giant — Archetype Totem 03

## One-line brief
A toppled gladiator-era stone statue on a pedestal still proudly inscribed with a glory year. Visual shorthand for the program whose trophies outlast its health.

## Visual family
Ligne-claire totem, warm-ink + amber on bone paper. Matches locked master plate (Anxious Dynasty).

## Full prompt used
```
Single standalone 1:1 square illustration — not a grid, not a contact sheet, not panels. One image only.

Match the uploaded totem_master.png style exactly: flat ligne-claire on bone paper #F3EEE4, uniform 2px warm-ink #0B0F14 contour line, flat fills only, no hatching, no shadow, no gradients. Clean mid-century European cartooning discipline.

SUBJECT: a toppled stone statue lying on its side on a short pedestal. The pedestal is still inscribed with a glory year in Roman numerals. Fine hairline cracks across the statue's torso. No humans visible — the statue itself is the object. No faces, no team logos.

AMBER: the inscribed Roman-numeral year on the pedestal is amber #E0A300. Exactly one amber detail.

COMPOSITION: object off-center right, negative space at left, ~20% paper margin on all sides.
```

## Reference images
- `assets/masters/totem_master.png` (uploaded to Art Director Custom GPT as canonical style anchor)

## Model + settings
- ChatGPT Pro, Art Director Custom GPT (gpt-image-1 backend)
- 1:1 aspect, single image per turn
- No curation — first keep.

## Selection rationale
Subject reads instantly at small size — recognizable as a fallen warrior figure with the glory year (MMXVII / 2017) serving as the amber anchor. The headless-torso framing reinforces the "wounded" identity without becoming gruesome. Off-center right composition honors the master plate.

## Rejection notes
None — single-image workflow, committed on first generation.
