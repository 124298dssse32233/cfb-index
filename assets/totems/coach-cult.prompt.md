# Coach Cult — Archetype Totem 12

## One-line brief
A cone-shaped megaphone with a faint halo hovering near it. Visual shorthand for the program whose entire identity is the guy yelling.

## Visual family
Ligne-claire totem, warm-ink + amber on bone paper. Matches locked master plate.

## Full prompt used
```
Single standalone 1:1 square illustration — not a grid, not a contact sheet, not panels. One image only.

Match the uploaded totem_master.png style exactly: flat ligne-claire on bone paper #F3EEE4, uniform 2px warm-ink contour, flat fills only, no hatching, no shadow, no gradients. Clean mid-century European cartooning discipline.

SUBJECT: a classic cone-shaped megaphone on its side with the bell pointing upper-right. A faint single-line halo hovers just above the narrow end of the megaphone. No humans, no team logos, no text.

AMBER: the halo line is amber #E0A300. Exactly one amber detail.

COMPOSITION: megaphone off-center right, halo above it, negative space at left, ~20% paper margin.
```

## Reference images
- `assets/masters/totem_master.png`

## Model + settings
- ChatGPT Pro, Art Director Custom GPT (gpt-image-1 backend), 1:1, single image.

## Selection rationale
Three-element read — the megaphone (authority), the halo (sanctification), the negative space (everything else the program doesn't have). The halo is thin, single-line, and floats with the gap between it and the megaphone doing the metaphor's work (the coach is close to divine but not quite claiming it). Bell opens upper-right, pushing the eye toward the "shout direction" which is off-canvas — implied audience. Minor composition drift: megaphone centers a little left of the master-plate rule. Accepted.

## Rejection notes
None — single-image workflow, committed on first generation.
