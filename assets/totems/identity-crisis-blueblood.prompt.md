# Identity-Crisis Blueblood — Archetype Totem 06

## One-line brief
A cracked wall-mirror with a generic helmet's reflection fractured across the glass. Visual shorthand for the blueblood program that no longer recognizes itself.

## Selection rationale
Reads instantly — cracked mirror + helmet fragment = program looking at a version of itself it doesn't recognize. The central impact point with radiating cracks anchors the eye exactly where it should. Amber hanging nail at the top is a small but crisp accent that doesn't fight the helmet for attention. Brown wooden frame acts as substrate-neutral, consistent with the clipboard and pennant-plank precedents.
