# Perpetual Believer — Archetype Totem 02

## One-line brief
A weathered "NEXT YEAR" pennant thumbtacked to a splintered plank. Visual shorthand for the archetype that has been promising next year for twenty years.

## Visual family
Ligne-claire totem, warm-ink + amber on bone paper. Matches locked master plate (Anxious Dynasty).

## Full prompt used
```
Single standalone 1:1 square illustration — not a grid, not a contact sheet, not panels. One image only.

Match the uploaded totem_master.png style exactly: flat ligne-claire on bone paper #F3EEE4, uniform 2px warm-ink #0B0F14 contour line on every shape, flat local-color fills only, no hatching, no cross-hatching, no shadow, no gloss, no gradients. Clean mid-century European cartooning discipline.

SUBJECT: a weathered cloth pennant reading "NEXT YEAR" in condensed serif caps, thumbtacked at one corner to a splintered wooden plank. Edges frayed. One corner drooping. No humans, no faces, no team logos.

AMBER: the thumbtack head is amber #E0A300. Exactly one amber detail in the entire image.

COMPOSITION: object off-center right, generous negative space at left, ~20% paper margin on all sides.
```

## Reference images
- `assets/masters/totem_master.png` (uploaded to Art Director Custom GPT as canonical style anchor)

## Model + settings
- ChatGPT Pro, Art Director Custom GPT (gpt-image-1 backend)
- 1:1 aspect, single image per turn
- No curation — first keep.

## Selection rationale
Deviation from prompt: pennant rendered in a muted brick-red rather than warm-ink neutral. Accepted — the red reads as a faded felt college pennant, which strengthens the nostalgia of "NEXT YEAR." The amber thumbtack remains the sole accent; the red is a substrate color, not a second accent. Off-center right composition + negative space at left matches master-plate rule.

## Rejection notes
None — single-image workflow, committed on first generation.
