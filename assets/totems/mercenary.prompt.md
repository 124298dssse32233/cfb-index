# Mercenary — Archetype Totem 14

## One-line brief
An open briefcase containing a playbook with X-and-O diagrams. Visual shorthand for the program that buys its identity at the portal.

## Visual family
Ligne-claire totem, warm-ink + amber on bone paper. Matches locked master plate.

## Full prompt used
```
Single standalone 1:1 square illustration — not a grid, not a contact sheet, not panels. One image only.

Match the uploaded totem_master.png style exactly: flat ligne-claire on bone paper #F3EEE4, uniform 2px warm-ink contour, flat fills only, no hatching, no shadow, no gradients. Clean mid-century European cartooning discipline.

SUBJECT: a hard-sided rectangular briefcase lying open. Inside, instead of documents, is a single playbook with visible X-and-O diagrams on the exposed page. The briefcase has a metal clasp and handle. No humans, no team logos.

AMBER: the briefcase's metal clasp is amber #E0A300. Exactly one amber detail.

COMPOSITION: briefcase off-center right, lid opened toward upper-left, negative space at left, ~20% paper margin.
```

## Reference images

## Model + settings
- ChatGPT Pro, Art Director Custom GPT (gpt-image-1 backend), 1:1, single image.

## Selection rationale
Open briefcase in 3/4 view, spiral-bound playbook visible inside with a full offensive formation diagram drawn in warm ink (full X-front, O-backfield, a run-arrow and a route-arrow). The amber clasp on the briefcase front is a small crisp accent. The spiral-binding detail reads more "actual playbook, not generic paper" — a good specificity choice. The "briefcase = money, playbook inside = scheme you rented" double metaphor lands.

## Rejection notes
None — single-image workflow, committed on first generation.
