## program_history

Clemson is the dynasty CFB wasn't supposed to have. The 2016-2019 four-year run includes 2 national titles, 2 finalist appearances, 55-4 record. Built by Dabo Swinney from a 2008 interim-coach handoff that almost no national-media outlet thought would matter.

## fanbase_summary

Clemson fans are blue-collar dynasty defenders. The town is small, the campus is rural, and yet the program out-recruits Florida State, beats Alabama in title games, and built the Saturday afternoon identity SEC programs spent decades cultivating.

## rivalry_context

South Carolina is the in-state nemesis (Clean Old-Fashioned Hate). Florida State is the ACC arch-rival, particularly in the Bobby Bowden / Tommy Bowden father-son era. The ACC-as-conference is the broader frame; Clemson IS the ACC's football identity since 2015.
