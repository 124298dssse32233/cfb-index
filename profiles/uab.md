## program_history

UAB football's defining narrative is the 2014 shutdown (only modern FBS program to be discontinued) and the 2017 resurrection. Bill Clark coached the final 2014 team, then returned to coach the 2017 resurrection. The 2018 11-3 + Conference USA championship + Boca Raton Bowl championship was the modern peak. Clark's 2022 medical retirement opened Trent Dilfer's 2023-present tenure. Protective Stadium (2021) modernized the venue.

## fanbase_summary

Blazer fans are Birmingham-Blazer-resurrection-loyalists. The 2014 shutdown / 2017 revival institutional memory + Bill Clark resurrection-coach narrative + Protective Stadium 2021 venue + Birmingham downtown setting + AAC conference identity (entering 2023) combine into a fanbase identity rooted in defiance + institutional revival.

## rivalry_context

Troy (the In-Alabama Sun Belt rival) + Memphis (the AAC era partner) + East Carolina + Tulane + the AAC cycle partners are the modern annual fixtures. The American Athletic realignment continues to develop rival cycles. Alabama / Auburn / Alabama-state-program-deference governs UAB's recruitment + identity positioning.
