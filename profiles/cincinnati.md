## program_history

Cincinnati football's modern peak was Luke Fickell's 2021 13-1 season — AAC championship, CFP appearance (the first ever for a Group of 5 program), semifinal loss to Alabama. The 2021 run produced multiple NFL draft picks (Sauce Gardner, Ahmad Gardner). Fickell's departure to Wisconsin (2023) brought Scott Satterfield to Cincinnati for the Big 12 transition era; competitive reality testing continues.

## fanbase_summary

Bearcat fans are Cincinnati-urban-loyalists. Nippert Stadium's on-campus urban setting concentrates the Saturday experience uniquely in CFB. The 2021 CFP run brought national-relevance fanbase identity that the Big 12 transition is testing.

## rivalry_context

Louisville (Keg of Nails) is the historic Big East-era rival; the cross-conference 2010s revival continues. Miami (OH) (Victory Bell) is the in-state Mid-American rival. The 2023 Big 12 move reshuffled all of them; current Big 12 partners are the new annual cycle.
