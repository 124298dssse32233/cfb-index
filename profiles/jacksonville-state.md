## program_history

Jacksonville State football's most-recent peak is Rich Rodriguez's 2024 9-4 + New Mexico Bowl championship — the program's first FBS bowl win, in the second year of Conference USA membership. Modern FCS peaks were John Grass's 2015 + 2017 FCS championship game appearances. The 2023 FBS reclassification + Conference USA entry was the most-recent FCS-to-FBS-conference ascent in CFB. The Gamecock mascot identity dates to 1947, predating South Carolina's mascot-identity-ownership by decades.

## fanbase_summary

Gamecock fans are Jacksonville-AL-Gamecock-loyalists. The Alabama-Gamecock-not-South-Carolina-Gamecock identity distinction + Rich Rodriguez coaching-name + 2024 New Mexico Bowl championship + Conference USA legitimacy combine into a fanbase identity defined by rapid ascent + national-narrative legitimacy.

## rivalry_context

Sam Houston (the FBS-newcomer cohort partner) is the structural Conference USA cycle game. UTEP + Western Kentucky + Liberty + other C-USA cycle partners are the new annual fixtures. The 2023 Conference USA entry continues to develop rival cycles. The Alabama-region geographic identity (Auburn / Alabama / Troy / South Alabama / UAB) creates a unique recruiting + fan-base catchment area.
