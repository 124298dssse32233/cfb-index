## program_history

Utah State football's modern peaks were Gary Andersen's 2012 11-2 + WAC championship + Famous Idaho Potato Bowl + #16 final AP and Blake Anderson's 2021 11-3 + Mountain West championship + LA Bowl championship. The 1888 founding + Maverik Stadium altitude + agricultural-college Utah State identity anchor the program. Bronco Mendenhall's 2024-present tenure continues the cycle.

## fanbase_summary

Aggie fans are Logan-Aggie-Big-Blue. The Cache Valley setting + Maverik Stadium altitude + agricultural-college institutional identity + Mountain West conference identity combine into a fanbase distinct from the larger Utah flagship-state programs (Utah, BYU).

## rivalry_context

BYU (the Wagon Wheel rivalry, since 1922, paused since 2010) is the In-State historical structural rival. Utah (the In-State Pac-12 rival) is the historic non-conference fixture. Wyoming + Boise State + Air Force are the modern Mountain West cycle annual partners. The MW realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
