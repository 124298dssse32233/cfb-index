## program_history

Hawai'i football's all-time peak was June Jones's 2007 12-1 + WAC championship + Sugar Bowl appearance + #10 final AP + Colt Brennan Heisman finalist. The Run-and-Shoot offense (Jones, 1999-2007) produced multiple NCAA passing records + Timmy Chang Heisman finalist. The 2020 Aloha Stadium closure forced Ching Athletics Complex (10,000 capacity) as temporary venue. Timmy Chang's 2022-present tenure (former QB, now head coach) continues the rebuild.
