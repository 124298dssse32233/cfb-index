## program_history

North Texas football's modern peak was Darrell Dickey's 2001-2004 4 consecutive Sun Belt championships + multiple bowl appearances. The 'Mean Green' identity originated with Joe Greene's 1966-1968 era (Hall of Fame NFL career with Pittsburgh Steelers). The 2011 Apogee Stadium opening + 2023 AAC entry (from C-USA) + Eric Morris's 2023-present tenure continue the modern era. The 2024 7-6 + bowl appearance is the most-recent peak.

## fanbase_summary

Mean Green fans are Denton-Mean-Green-GMG-loyalists. The Joe Greene historical anchor + Dickey 4-title dynasty + Apogee Stadium modern venue + Denton Texas geographic catchment + AAC conference identity combine into a fanbase distinct from larger Texas flagship programs.

## rivalry_context

UTEP (the historic C-USA cycle rival) + UTSA + Texas State + the AAC cycle partners are the modern annual fixtures. The American Athletic realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
