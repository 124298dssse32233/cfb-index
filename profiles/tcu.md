## program_history

TCU football's modern peak is the 2022 Sonny Dykes Cinderella run — 13-2, CFP semifinal win over Michigan, national championship game appearance vs Georgia. The 1930s-era Sammy Baugh and Davey O'Brien produced two Heismans and TCU's first national-relevance era; the 2010s Gary Patterson era stabilized the program in the Big 12 with several top-10 finishes. Dykes' Sugar Bowl + title-game run is the modern reset.

## fanbase_summary

Horned Frog fans are Fort-Worth-purple-loyalists. Hypnotoad as the Twitter fan-voice is the most-replicable meme in CFB. Amon G. Carter Stadium tailgates skew younger and more cosmopolitan than typical Big 12 standard; the 2022 run brought back generational alumni for every home game.

## rivalry_context

SMU (the Iron Skillet) is the historic intra-state rival the AAC/Big 12 split muddled. Baylor (in-state Big 12) is the structural rival. Texas and Oklahoma's 2024 exits to the SEC reshaped Big 12 competitive dynamics — TCU is now positioned as a senior Big 12 power program.
