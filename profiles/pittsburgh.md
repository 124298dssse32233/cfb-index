## program_history

Pittsburgh football's all-time peak was 1976 — Tony Dorsett's Heisman season + the program's most-recent claimed national title under Johnny Majors. The 1980s Dan Marino era (1979-1982) produced #1 rankings and the modern brand recovery. The post-Marino three decades were the wilderness era; Pat Narduzzi's 2021 ACC championship (and his ongoing 2015-present tenure) is the structural reset.

## fanbase_summary

Panther fans are Steel-City-loyalist-patient. Pittsburgh's identity as a Steelers city sometimes overshadows the football program; the 2021 ACC title brought BBN-style civic-pride return. Acrisure Stadium (shared with the NFL) sells out for big games but not every Saturday.

## rivalry_context

West Virginia (Backyard Brawl) is the defining rivalry — 125+ years, 60-mile geography, state-pride stakes. Penn State (post-realignment dormant) was the historical East Coast Big East-era rival. Notre Dame (occasional) and Miami (ACC partner) are the modern annual fixtures.
