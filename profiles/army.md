## program_history

Army football's modern peak is the 2024 12-2 + Commander-in-Chief's Trophy under Jeff Monken. The all-time peak was Earl Blaik's 1944-1946 era — Doc Blanchard (1945 Heisman) + Glenn Davis (1946 Heisman) + 27-0-1 three-year record + multiple national titles. The post-WWII service-academy ceiling has flexed across eras; Monken's tenure brought back competitive cycles.

## fanbase_summary

Army fans are West-Point-Corps-loyalists. The Cadet corps + alumni network + military-family geographic dispersion create a fanbase that values service-academy mission alignment over conventional wins-only loyalty. Every senior playing their last game vs Navy is the defining annual emotional arc.

## rivalry_context

Navy (the Army-Navy game, since 1890) is the rivalry — one of CFB's most-storied. Air Force completes the service-academy triangle for the Commander-in-Chief's Trophy. The Independent / American Athletic shifts changed conference partners; the service-academy axis remains permanent.
