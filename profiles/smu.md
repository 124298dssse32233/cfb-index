## program_history

SMU football's two peak eras frame the program: the 1979-1982 Pony Express (Eric Dickerson + Craig James, 1982 11-0-1, two future Hall of Famers) and the 2024 11-3 + ACC entry + CFP appearance under Rhett Lashlee. Between them: the 1987 NCAA death penalty (the only one ever issued), no football in 1987, voluntary cancellation in 1988, and a decades-long rebuild through Group of 5 obscurity. The 2024 cycle is the resurrection completed.

## fanbase_summary

Mustang fans are Dallas-Pony-Up-survivors. The Pony Express legacy + death-penalty institutional memory + 2024 ACC entry combined create a fanbase identity that values resilience and reinvention. The Highland Park / Dallas demographic + NIL infrastructure positioned SMU for the P5 transition.

## rivalry_context

TCU (the in-state Big 12 rival by proximity — the Battle for the Iron Skillet) is the historic Texas fixture. Houston (American Athletic era) was the structural conference rival. Rice (the oldest annual rival, since 1916) is the cross-conference historical fixture. The 2024 ACC entry reshaped the rivalry calendar; current ACC partners are the new annual cycle.
