## program_history

Tulsa football's modern peaks were Todd Graham's 2010 10-3 + Conference USA championship + Hawaii Bowl championship and Bill Blankenship's 2012 11-3 + Liberty Bowl championship. The Air Raid offense identity (continuing from the Graham era through Philip Montgomery + Kevin Wilson eras) is permanent program scheme. Tre Lamb's 2024-present tenure continues the cycle.

## fanbase_summary

Golden Hurricane fans are Tulsa-Hurricane-Reign-Cane-loyalists. The Oklahoma G5 alternative + Skelly Field at H.A. Chapman Stadium + Air Raid offense identity + Reign Cane brand identity + Tulsa metropolitan-area geographic catchment combine into a fanbase distinct from the dominant Oklahoma flagship-state programs (OU + OSU).

## rivalry_context

Tulsa-Arkansas (the historic non-conference rival) + Memphis (the AAC era partner) + Houston (the AAC era partner) are the modern annual fixtures. The American Athletic conference realignment continues to develop rival cycles. The Oklahoma flagship-state programs (OU + OSU) are wishlist non-conference targets but unattainable in current scheduling era.
