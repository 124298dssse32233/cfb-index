## program_history

Miami's 1983-2001 run produced 5 national championships, defined the modern celebrity-football aesthetic, and gave CFB its most-cited cultural artifact (the 2001 Cane teams — possibly the greatest CFB roster ever assembled). The 2010s collapse was structural (NCAA sanctions, ACC realignment isolation). Cristobal's 2022 hire is the rebuild bet.

## fanbase_summary

Miami fans are renegade-romantics. The 1980s identity is the canonical reference; the 2020s identity is still being negotiated. The fanbase is small in raw size compared to SEC blue-bloods, but loud-per-fan in a way that makes the stadium feel bigger than it is.

## rivalry_context

Florida State is the historical SEC-rival-by-proxy (both ACC, both Florida, both 1980s-90s dynasties). Florida is the in-state grudge that's been off-cycle for two decades. The 2024 Florida game restarting the series is the storyline through 2027.
