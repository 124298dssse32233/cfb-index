## program_history

Texas State football's modern peak was GJ Kinne's 2023 8-5 + First Responder Bowl championship — the program's first FBS bowl win. The 2012 FBS reclassification opened the modern era; the 2013-2022 transitional period struggled. Kinne's 2023-present tenure + the youth-coach methodology + recruiting reach into Texas opened the modern ascent.

## fanbase_summary

Bobcat fans are San-Marcos-Bobcat-Eat-Em-Up. The 2012 FBS reclassification + 'Eat 'Em Up Cats' fight song + San Marcos Texas geographic catchment + Sun Belt conference identity + GJ Kinne 2023 breakthrough combine into a fanbase distinct from larger Texas flagship-state programs.

## rivalry_context

UTSA (the Battle for Interstate 35) is the In-Texas Sun Belt rival. App State + Coastal Carolina + Marshall are the Sun Belt cycle annual partners. The Sun Belt realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
