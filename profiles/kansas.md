## program_history

Kansas football's all-time peak is Mark Mangino's 2007 12-1 + Orange Bowl championship — the only major bowl win in modern Jayhawk history. Modern peak is Lance Leipold's 2021-present rebuild from 1-11 (the 2020 floor under Les Miles) to 6-7 + bowl eligibility in 2022, then 9-4 + bowl win in 2023. Jalon Daniels' Heisman buzz drove national narrative. The basketball-school identity (Bill Self era + multiple national titles) is the cultural anchor; football is the contrast narrative.

## fanbase_summary

Jayhawk fans are Lawrence-Rock-Chalk-loyalists. The Rock Chalk chant + David Booth Kansas Memorial Stadium + the basketball/football identity contrast + the Leipold rebuild thesis combine into a fanbase that learned to value football wins as exceptional rather than expected.

## rivalry_context

Missouri (the Border War, paused since 2011 SEC departure) was the historic structural rival, awaiting potential revival. Kansas State (the Sunflower Showdown) is the in-state Big 12 fixture. Iowa State (the structural Big 12 cycle partner) is the modern annual game. The Big 12 realignment shuffles continue to reshape rival cycles.
