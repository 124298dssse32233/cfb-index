## program_history

Wisconsin is the Big Ten's blue-collar identity made literal. Barry Alvarez's 1990s rebuild produced 3 Rose Bowl wins (1994, 1999, 2000). Bret Bielema and Paul Chryst sustained the running-game cathedral through the 2010s. The 2024 transition era is testing whether the identity persists into the NIL/portal era.

## fanbase_summary

Badger fans are hard-work cheeseheads. They eat brats, drink Spotted Cow, and treat the offensive line like skill players. Camp Randall is the Big Ten's loudest stadium that isn't Penn State; Jump Around is the Big Ten's most-replicated 4th-quarter moment. Patient and proud.

## rivalry_context

Minnesota (Paul Bunyan's Axe) is the rivalry that defines the calendar — the oldest trophy game in CFB. Iowa is the divisional twin that mirrors Wisconsin's identity. Ohio State is the conference's structural blocker; the 2024 Big Ten realignment matters most because of how often the path to Indianapolis runs through Columbus.
