## program_history

UNLV football's modern peak was the 2024 11-3 + Mountain West championship game appearance under Dan Mullen (transitioning from Barry Odom's 2023 9-5 + LA Bowl appearance reset). All-time peak was Harvey Hyde's 1984 11-2 + California Bowl championship. The 2020 Allegiant Stadium opening (Raiders' new home) gave UNLV an NFL-grade venue. Las Vegas market + NIL infrastructure drive the modern era. Dan Mullen's 2024-present tenure continues the trajectory.

## fanbase_summary

Rebel fans are Vegas-Rebel-Rebel-Up. The Allegiant Stadium NFL-shared venue + Las Vegas Strip proximity + casino-corridor cultural overlap + Mountain West conference identity + Barry Odom + Dan Mullen narrative combine into a fanbase distinct from larger Nevada / regional programs.

## rivalry_context

Nevada (the Battle for Nevada / Fremont Cannon, since 1969) is the In-State Mountain West structural rival. Boise State + San Diego State + Fresno State + Air Force are the Mountain West cycle annual partners. The Mountain West realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
