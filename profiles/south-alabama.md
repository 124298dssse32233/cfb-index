## program_history

South Alabama football was founded in 2009 — one of the most-recent FBS-level program creations in CFB history. Joey Jones (2009-2015) built the program through FCS independent → FBS independent → Sun Belt 2013 entry. Steve Campbell (2018-2020) struggled. Kane Wommack's 2021-2024 tenure produced the program's modern peak (2023 7-6 + 68 Ventures Bowl appearance). Major Applewhite's 2025-present tenure continues the cycle. The 2020 Hancock Whitney Stadium opening modernized the venue.

## fanbase_summary

Jaguar fans are Mobile-Jaguar-founding-era. The 2009 program-founding institutional memory + Mobile Gulf Coast geographic catchment + Senior Bowl host city proximity + Hancock Whitney Stadium dedicated venue + Sun Belt conference identity combine into a fanbase distinct from larger Alabama flagship-state programs (Alabama, Auburn).

## rivalry_context

Troy (the Sun Belt structural rival, the Belt of the South) is the In-Alabama Sun Belt fixture. App State + Coastal Carolina + Marshall + Georgia State + James Madison are the Sun Belt cycle annual partners. The Sun Belt realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
