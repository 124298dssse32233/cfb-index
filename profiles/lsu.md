## program_history

LSU football is the SEC's nocturnal cathedral. Founded 1893, peaks every two decades or so, and reorganizes the national conversation when it does. 2019 is the most recent peak — Burrow's Heisman, 15-0, an offense that broke the conference's defensive ceiling.

## fanbase_summary

LSU fans are bayou cathedral-builders: they treat the stadium as sacred space and the team's performance as collective worship. The night home games are the festival; the away games are the diaspora. No fanbase in CFB does louder, drunker, more unified Saturday nights.

## rivalry_context

Alabama is the standard. Ole Miss is the cousin. Arkansas is the rude neighbor. The SEC West cross-divisional alignment confused this; the SEC's east-west collapse in 2024 reclarified it.
