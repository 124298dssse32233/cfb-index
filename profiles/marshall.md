## program_history

Marshall football's defining historical anchor is the 1970 plane crash (75 killed) and the 5-year rebuild. The modern peak was Bob Pruett's 1996-2004 tenure (six MAC championships, multiple top-25 finishes, Randy Moss + Chad Pennington development). The 2024 Sun Belt championship under Charles Huff continued the multi-era ascent narrative. 'We Are Marshall' (2006 film) made the 1970 story national canon. The annual memorial service at the fountain (where the names are inscribed) is permanent ritual.

## fanbase_summary

Thundering Herd fans are Huntington-Herd-survivors. The 1970 plane-crash institutional memory + 'We Are Marshall' film + Bob Pruett dynasty + Randy Moss / Chad Pennington star power + 2024 Sun Belt championship combine into a fanbase identity rooted in resurrection + remembrance. Joan C. Edwards Stadium + the campus memorial fountain anchor every game-day visual.

## rivalry_context

Ohio (the Battle of the Bell, since 1905) is the historic MAC rival, paused with conference changes. App State (the In-State Mountain rival from Sun Belt) is the structural conference fixture. The 2024 Sun Belt championship era's rival cycle includes James Madison + Coastal Carolina as new annual partners. The MAC-to-Sun Belt transition continues to reshape.
