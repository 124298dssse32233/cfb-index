## program_history

Virginia Tech football's peak was the 1995-2010 Frank Beamer era — 12 straight bowl games (1995-2006), the 1999 BCS title-game appearance with freshman Michael Vick, the Big East championships, and the eventual ACC integration in 2004. "Beamer Ball" — special-teams-and-defense as offense — is the program's permanent stylistic identity. Brent Pry's 2022 hire is the modern reset.

## fanbase_summary

Hokie fans are Lunch-Pail-loyalists. The 65,000-strong Enter Sandman jump at Lane Stadium remains the program's defining visual. Blacksburg's geographic isolation (rural southwest Virginia) concentrates the fanbase's pride; the diaspora is unusually loyal to alma mater traditions.

## rivalry_context

Virginia (Commonwealth Cup) is the in-state structural rival. West Virginia (Black Diamond Trophy) is the historic regional rival the conference realignments dismantled. Miami is the post-2004 ACC blocker. Pittsburgh and Boston College are the longer-tenured ACC partners.
