## program_history

Coastal Carolina football's modern peak was Jamey Chadwell's 2020 11-1 + #14 final AP rank + Sun Belt East division championship — coming off a 2017 FBS reclassification, the most-recent G5 program to make the FCS-to-FBS-conference-relevance ascent. The 2020 missed-Cure-Bowl-due-to-COVID-positive-tests drama was the only blemish on the perfect-conference-record season. Chadwell's 2022 departure to Liberty opened the Tim Beck era. The teal turf (2015) is permanent visual identity.

## fanbase_summary

Chanticleer fans are Conway-teal-Chanticleer-loyalists. The teal-turf venue + Chanticleer literary-origin mascot + 2020 #14 AP finish + Jamey Chadwell ascent narrative + Myrtle Beach proximity combine into a fanbase identity distinct from larger flagship-state programs. Brooks Stadium's teal-on-the-field aesthetic is uniquely Coastal Carolina.

## rivalry_context

App State (the Sun Belt era structural rival) is the modern annual cycle game. Liberty (the Jamey Chadwell continuity rival, where Chadwell built his next reputation) is the recurring cycle. The Sun Belt realignment continues to develop new rival cycles. The geographic proximity to Myrtle Beach + Charleston / Wilmington markets give the program a unique recruiting + fan-base catchment area.
