## program_history

Temple football's modern peak was Matt Rhule's 2016 10-4 + AAC championship + Military Bowl appearance. All-time peak was Wayne Hardin's 1979 10-2 + Garden State Bowl + #17 final AP rank. Rhule's 2013-2016 tenure + the program-building methodology + 2017 departure to Baylor (then Carolina Panthers, Nebraska) gave Temple permanent national-narrative weight. Stan Drayton's 2022-present tenure continues the rebuild. Lincoln Financial Field (2003+) is the shared NFL venue.

## fanbase_summary

Owl fans are Philadelphia-Owl-Temple-Made. The Lincoln Financial Field shared NFL stadium + Matt Rhule narrative + 'Temple Made' mantra + AAC conference identity + Philadelphia metropolitan-area split-loyalty + academic institutional pride combine into a fanbase distinct from larger Northeast professional-sports markets.

## rivalry_context

Penn State (the historic non-conference rival) is the long-running annual fixture, paused with conference realignment. Connecticut (the AAC era partner) is the modern cycle game. East Carolina + Tulane + Memphis are the AAC cycle annual partners. The American Athletic realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
