## program_history

Middle Tennessee football's modern era is defined by Rick Stockstill's 2006-2024 tenure — the longest sustained head-coach run in program history. Multiple bowl appearances + Conference USA championships + sustained G5 competitive cycles. The 2018 8-6 + Hawaii Bowl championship + 2009 10-3 + New Orleans Bowl appearance are the era's peaks. The 1933 Floyd Stadium opening + multiple expansions anchor the on-campus venue. Derek Mason's 2025-present tenure continues the rebuild post-Stockstill.

## fanbase_summary

Blue Raider fans are Murfreesboro-Blue-Raider-Raider-Up. The Floyd Stadium 1933 venue + Stockstill 18-year tenure + Conference USA conference identity + Murfreesboro Tennessee geographic catchment combine into a fanbase distinct from larger Tennessee flagship-state programs (Tennessee, Vanderbilt, Memphis).

## rivalry_context

Western Kentucky (the 100 Miles of Hate, since 1936) is the historic Conference USA cycle rival. UAB + UTEP + the C-USA cycle partners are the modern annual fixtures. The C-USA realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
