## program_history

Arizona State football's modern peak is Kenny Dillingham's 2024 11-3 + Big 12 championship + first-ever CFP appearance — coming off a 3-9 floor in 2023, one of the most-improbable single-season turnarounds in CFB. Historical peak was Bruce Snyder's 1996 11-1 + Rose Bowl + #4 AP finish (Jake Plummer QB). The Pac-12 to Big 12 conference transition (2024) anchored the new competitive era. Cam Skattebo's Heisman-finalist 2024 added star-narrative.

## fanbase_summary

Sun Devil fans are Tempe-pitchfork-loyalists. The Fork Up gesture + Sparky mascot + maroon-and-gold + 2024 CFP run combined into a fanbase identity that survived the Pac-12 collapse and emerged stronger in the Big 12. Dillingham's age-34 youngest-P5-coach narrative drives national recruiting credibility.

## rivalry_context

Arizona (the Territorial Cup, since 1899 — one of CFB's oldest trophy games) is the structural in-state rival, now both in Big 12. UCLA / USC (the Pac-12 era structural fixtures) were lost to the 2024 realignment. The Big 12 cycle replaced them with Texas Tech / Baylor / TCU as annual fixtures.
