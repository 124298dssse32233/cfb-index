## program_history

Iowa football is Kirk Ferentz's 27-year (and counting) cathedral to consistency. Since 1999, the program has produced 7+ wins in 18 of 25 seasons, an offense-or-defense-coordinator promotion cycle, and the rare CFB phenomenon of a head coach who's been at one school longer than most fans of that school have been alive.

## fanbase_summary

Hawkeye fans are stoic heartland loyalists. They show up to Kinnick on cold November afternoons in numbers that don't change for the weather. The Wave (since 2017) is the spiritual center; the punt block is the technical center. Patient with everything except offensive coordinator hires.

## rivalry_context

Iowa State (Cy-Hawk Trophy) is the in-state rival. Minnesota (Floyd of Rosedale, a literal bronze pig) is the Black Friday divisional twin. Wisconsin is the structural conference rival both teams ignore until the standings make it relevant.
