## program_history

Northwestern football's modern peak runs across two eras — Gary Barnett's 1995 10-2 + Big Ten championship + Rose Bowl (the program's first since 1936) and Pat Fitzgerald's 2006-2022 tenure (5 division titles, 2 Big Ten title game appearances). The 2023 firing (hazing scandal) ended Fitzgerald's tenure abruptly; David Braun's interim-then-permanent transition continues the program's modern era. The 2024-2026 Ryan Field reconstruction sent home games to a temporary lakefront stadium.

## fanbase_summary

Wildcat fans are Evanston-academic-elite-Wildcats. The academic prestige + Big Ten conference identity + Lake Michigan campus geography combine into a fanbase that values institutional alignment over wins-only loyalty. The Fitzgerald-era competitive wins normalized higher expectations.

## rivalry_context

Illinois (the Land of Lincoln Trophy, the in-state Big Ten rival) is the structural fixture. Iowa (the Big Ten West historical rival) is the structural division partner. The post-Big-Ten-West-realignment shuffles changed the conference calendar; current Big Ten partners are the new annual fixtures.
