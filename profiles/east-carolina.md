## program_history

East Carolina football's modern peak was Skip Holtz's 2008-2009 era — particularly the 2008 8-5 + Conference USA championship + Liberty Bowl championship. Modern peak rebuild is Blake Harrell's 2024 8-5 + bowl eligibility (Harrell's first year as head coach after Mike Houston's exit). The Purple Haze stadium aesthetic (Dowdy-Ficklen since 1963) + Pirate Cannon (since 1985) anchor permanent program identity. The Conference USA → American Athletic transition shaped the modern era.

## fanbase_summary

Pirate fans are Greenville-Pirate-Nation. The eastern-North-Carolina geographic catchment + Purple Haze venue aesthetic + Pirate Cannon tradition + AAC conference identity + Skip Holtz 2008 peak combine into a fanbase distinct from larger North Carolina programs (NC State + UNC + Duke + Wake Forest).

## rivalry_context

NC State (the In-State Tobacco Road-adjacent rival) is the structural North Carolina fixture (paused, with intermittent meetings). Cincinnati (the AAC era rival, now Big 12) is the lost-to-realignment fixture. The 2024 AAC reshuffle continues to develop annual rival cycles. Memphis + Tulane + other AAC partners are the modern annual fixtures.
