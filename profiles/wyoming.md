## program_history

Wyoming football's all-time peak was Lloyd Eaton's 1967 10-1 + Sugar Bowl appearance + #6 final AP. Modern peak era was Craig Bohl's 2014-2023 tenure (multiple bowl appearances, Josh Allen development pipeline). The Josh Allen 2014-2017 era + 2018 #7 NFL draft pick + Buffalo Bills MVP-contender career retroactively elevated Wyoming's recruiting profile. Jay Sawvel's 2024-present tenure continues the cycle.

## fanbase_summary

Cowboy fans are Laramie-Cowboy-tough. The 7,220 ft War Memorial Stadium altitude + frontier-cowboy identity + bucking-horse-and-rider state logo + Mountain West conference identity combine into a fanbase distinct from the larger Mountain West programs.

## rivalry_context

Colorado State (Bronze Boot Trophy, since 1968) is the structural Mountain West fixture. Air Force (the historic Mountain West cycle rival) is the recurring annual game. Boise State + Utah State + Fresno State are the modern Mountain West cycle annual partners. The MW realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
