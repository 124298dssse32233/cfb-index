## washington-state football's modern peaks span Mike Price's 1997 10-2 + Rose Bowl + #9 AP (Ryan Leaf QB) and Mike Leach's 2018 11-2 + Alamo Bowl appearance. Leach's tenure (2012-2019) cemented the Air Raid as permanent identity. Leach's 2019 departure + Nick Rolovich's brief tenure + Jake Dickert's 2021-present rebuild + 2024 Pac-12-collapse holdout transitional year define the modern era. Leach's 2022 death at Mississippi State was mourned as a Cougar loss.

## fanbase_summary

Cougar fans are Pullman-Coug-holdouts. The Pullman remoteness (most-isolated P5 campus) + Mike Leach Air Raid legacy + 2024 holdout defiance combine into a fanbase identity rooted in survival + persistence. The Apple Cup rivalry with Washington is the structural annual anchor.

## rivalry_context

Washington (the Apple Cup, since 1900) is the structural in-state rival. Oregon State (the other Pac-12 holdout, the 'Pac-12 Two' fixture) is the partnership-survivor rival. Mountain West fixtures (Boise State + Air Force + Fresno State + others) are the 2024 transitional schedule. The Pac-12 legal entity's status governs the conference identity going forward.
