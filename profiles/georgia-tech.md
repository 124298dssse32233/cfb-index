## program_history

Georgia Tech football's all-time peak was Bobby Ross's 1990 11-0-1 + UPI national championship (split with Colorado in the AP poll). Four national titles in program history (1917 / 1928 / 1952 / 1990). Modern peaks include Chan Gailey's early 2000s era, Paul Johnson's 2008-2018 triple-option era (2014 Orange Bowl win), and Brent Key's 2024 7-6 rebuild. The 2014 Georgia upset ('fumble heard round Athens') + the 2024 8-OT thriller produced peak rivalry moments.

## fanbase_summary

Yellow Jacket fans are Atlanta-engineers-Yellow-Jackets. The Ramblin' Wreck Model A + 'I'm a Ramblin' Wreck' fight song (since 1908) + Bobby Dodd Stadium urban-Atlanta location + engineering-school + ACC identity combine into a fanbase that values heritage + Clean Old-Fashioned Hate.

## rivalry_context

Georgia (Clean Old-Fashioned Hate, since 1893) is the structural in-state rivalry. Clemson (the ACC era fixture) is the modern annual game. Florida State (the historic ACC fixture) is the long-running annual cycle. The ACC realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
