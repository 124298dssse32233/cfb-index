## program_history

Arkansas football's 1964 national championship under Frank Broyles is the program's only major national peak — a 11-0 season that predated the BCS era. Modern peaks: Houston Nutt's 1998 SEC title game, Bobby Petrino's 2010-2011 top-5 finishes (before the motorcycle-and-administrative-assistant scandal), Bret Bielema's 2014-2015 SEC competitiveness. Sam Pittman's 2020-2023 stabilization preceded the 2024 transitional season.

## fanbase_summary

Razorback fans are diaspora-loyalist-passionate. Arkansas has the only Power-5 football program in the state, so the fanbase concentrates regional pride more than any other SEC program. The Hog call travels with alumni to road games in densities that surprise visiting fanbases.

## rivalry_context

LSU (Battle for the Boot) is the structural rival via geography and SEC West history. Texas A&M (Southwest Classic at AT&T Stadium, neutral site) is the post-SEC-expansion rivalry. Missouri is the newer SEC East rivalry. The 2024 SEC divisionless realignment is reshaping all of them.
