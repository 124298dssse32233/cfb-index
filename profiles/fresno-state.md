## program_history

Fresno State football's modern peaks span Pat Hill's 1997-2011 'Anyone, Anytime, Anywhere' era + Jeff Tedford's 2018 12-2 + Mountain West championship + Las Vegas Bowl championship. All-time peak was Jim Sweeney's 1985 11-0-1 + California Bowl championship + #16 final AP. The Carr brothers QB pipeline (David #1 NFL draft 2002, Derek 2014) cemented Fresno State as G5 QB development institution. Jeff Tedford's 2022 departure to UC opened Jeff Tedford's return then Mike Sanford eras.

## fanbase_summary

Bulldog fans are Fresno-Bulldog-loyalists. The Central California Valley geographic catchment + Pat Hill 'Anyone, Anytime, Anywhere' philosophy + Carr-brothers QB pipeline + 2018 Tedford Mountain West championship combine into a fanbase identity distinct from larger California flagship-state programs. The Mountain West conference identity + sustained competitive cycles anchor program positioning.

## rivalry_context

San Diego State (the Mountain West cycle partner) is the structural conference fixture. Boise State (the Mountain West powerhouse rival) is the historic G5 ceiling competition. The Mountain West realignment continues to develop rival cycles. The historic California State University System (San Jose State + Cal Poly + others) creates intra-state regional rivalries.
