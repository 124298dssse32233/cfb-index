## program_history

San Diego State football's modern peak was Rocky Long's 2016 11-3 + Mountain West championship + Las Vegas Bowl championship. All-time peak was Don Coryell's 1969 11-0 + Pasadena Bowl + #18 final AP. Coryell's 1961-1972 tenure pioneered the passing-offense identity that later defined his Chargers 'Air Coryell' NFL offense. Long's 2009-2019 tenure + 3-3-5 defensive scheme produced multiple Mountain West championships. Brady Hoke's return tenure (2020-2024) bridged eras. The 2022 Snapdragon Stadium opening anchored the modern era.

## fanbase_summary

Aztec fans are San-Diego-Aztec-believers. The 'I Believe' chant + Snapdragon Stadium dedicated-football venue + Mountain West conference identity + Don Coryell historical anchor + Rocky Long 3-3-5 legacy combine into a fanbase distinct from larger flagship-state programs. The San Diego market split-loyalty (vs USC / UCLA / Cal) + the dedicated G5 fanbase create a unique recruiting + fan-base catchment area.

## rivalry_context

Fresno State (the Mountain West historical rival) is the structural conference fixture. UNLV (the Mountain West cycle partner) is the recurring annual game. Boise State (the Mountain West powerhouse rival) is the historic G5 ceiling competition. The Mountain West realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
