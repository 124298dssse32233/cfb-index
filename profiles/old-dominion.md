## program_history

Old Dominion football's defining historical narrative is the 68-year gap — football dropped 1941, revived 2009 under Bobby Wilder. The program's modern ascent: FCS 2009, FBS reclassification 2014, multiple bowl appearances, 2016 Bahamas Bowl championship (Wilder's peak), Sun Belt membership. Wilder's 2018 retirement + the Rahne family + Ricky Rahne's 2020-present tenure continue the rebuild. S.B. Ballard Stadium (2019) modernized the facility.

## fanbase_summary

Monarch fans are Norfolk-Monarch-resurrection-loyalists. The 1941-2009 football gap + 2009 resurrection + Bobby Wilder narrative + Norfolk Tidewater geographic catchment + Sun Belt conference identity combine into a fanbase identity rooted in institutional revival.

## rivalry_context

App State + Coastal Carolina + Marshall + Georgia Southern are the Sun Belt cycle annual partners. James Madison (the in-Virginia regional rival, also FBS newcomer) is the recurring annual cycle game. The Sun Belt realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
