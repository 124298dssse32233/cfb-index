## program_history

Boston College football's all-time peak was Jack Bicknell's 1984 10-2 + Cotton Bowl championship + Doug Flutie Heisman. The November 23, 1984 Hail Mary vs Miami (47-45) is permanent CFB canon. Modern peak was Matt Ryan's 2007 11-3 + ACC Atlantic championship era. Bill O'Brien's 2024-present tenure continues the cycle. Alumni Stadium (built 1957, expanded multiple times) is the program's permanent venue.

## fanbase_summary

Eagle fans are Chestnut-Hill-Eagle-For-Boston. The Catholic-private academic identity + Boston metropolitan-area split-loyalty + Doug Flutie historical anchor + 'For Boston' fight song + ACC conference identity combine into a fanbase distinct from larger New England professional-sports markets.

## rivalry_context

Notre Dame (the historic Catholic-Catholic rivalry, the Frank Leahy Memorial Bowl) is the most-storied non-conference fixture. Miami (the Flutie Hail Mary opponent) + Florida State + Clemson are the historic ACC fixtures. The Big East / ACC realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
