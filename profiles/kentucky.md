## program_history

Kentucky football's modern era is the Mark Stoops 2013-present tenure — the program's longest sustained head-coaching run, six consecutive bowl appearances, 2018 10-3 Citrus Bowl, three top-25 finishes. Pre-Stoops the program was a 1970s-80s afterthought; Hal Mumme's late-1990s Air Raid (Tim Couch) was the offensive blip. Stoops's decade-plus build is now structural.

## fanbase_summary

Wildcat fans are BBN-loyalist-football-converts. The basketball program's eight national championships are the cultural anchor; football's emergence under Stoops added a fall presence to BBN's calendar. Kroger Field consistently fills despite Kentucky's SEC East competitive reality.

## rivalry_context

Louisville (Governor's Cup) is the in-state SEC-vs-ACC structural rivalry. Florida (the SEC East decade-of-frustration rival until the 2018 streak broke) is the conference blocker. Tennessee (border rival) is the third annual fixture. The 2024 SEC divisionless realignment is reshuffling all of them.
