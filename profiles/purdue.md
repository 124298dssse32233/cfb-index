## program_history

Purdue football's modern peak was Joe Tiller's 2000 8-1 + Big Ten championship + Rose Bowl run (Drew Brees as Heisman finalist). Second-tier peak was Jeff Brohm's 2022 9-4 + Big Ten East division + Big Ten Championship Game appearance. The 'Cradle of Quarterbacks' identity (14 NFL starting QBs developed) is the permanent program signature. Recent head coaches: Joe Tiller → Danny Hope → Darrell Hazell → Jeff Brohm → Ryan Walters.

## fanbase_summary

Boilermaker fans are West-Lafayette-engineering-loyalists. The land-grant + engineering-school identity + Cradle of QBs heritage + Big Ten conference fixture combine into a fanbase distinct from the larger flagship-state programs. The Old Oaken Bucket vs Indiana is the annual cultural anchor.

## rivalry_context

Indiana (Old Oaken Bucket, since 1925) is the in-state Big Ten rival. Notre Dame (the historic non-conference rival, the Shillelagh Trophy since 1958) is the most-storied non-Big-Ten fixture. The Big Ten realignment shuffled all of them; current Big Ten partners are the new annual cycle.
