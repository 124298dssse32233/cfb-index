## program_history

Florida International football began in 2002. Don Strock (2002-2006) was the founding head coach. Mario Cristobal's 2007-2012 era produced multiple bowl appearances. Butch Davis's 2017-2021 tenure produced the modern peak (2018 9-4 + Bahamas Bowl championship). Mike MacIntyre's 2022-present tenure continues the rebuild.

## fanbase_summary

Panther fans are Miami-Panther-FIU-loyalists. The 2002 founding institutional memory + Mario Cristobal narrative + Pitbull Stadium venue + Miami metropolitan-area split-loyalty + C-USA conference identity combine into a fanbase distinct from larger Florida flagship programs.

## rivalry_context

Florida Atlantic (the Shula Bowl) is the structural in-Florida G5 rival. UTEP + WKU + the C-USA cycle partners are the modern annual fixtures. The C-USA realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
