## program_history

Boise State football's modern peak runs across two eras — the 2007 Fiesta Bowl under Chris Petersen (the Statue of Liberty + 13-0 perfect season) and the 2024 CFP appearance under Spencer Danielson (11-2 regular season, Ashton Jeanty Heisman finalist, first-ever G5 CFP). The blue turf (installed 1986) is the program's permanent visual identity. Coaching pipeline: Chris Petersen → Bryan Harsin → Spencer Danielson.

## fanbase_summary

Bronco fans are Boise-blue-turf-true-believers. The blue turf as recruiting weapon + visual identity marker + national-TV signature has produced a fanbase that travels disproportionately for a G5 program. Reclamation of "smurf" as identity is unique in CFB.

## rivalry_context

Fresno State (the Mountain West rivalry by proximity + competitive parity) is the structural conference rival. BYU (the historic Mountain West / WAC rival, now P5 via Big 12) is the lost-to-realignment fixture. Air Force is the in-region service-academy fixture. The 2024 CFP appearance changed the program's positioning across every G5 rivalry.
