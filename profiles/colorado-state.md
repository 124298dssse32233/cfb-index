## program_history

Colorado State football's all-time peaks were Sonny Lubick's 1994 10-2 + #16 AP and 1997 11-2 + Holiday Bowl championship. Lubick's 1993-2007 tenure produced multiple Mountain West (then WAC) championships + sustained competitive cycles. The football complex is named for him. Jay Norvell's 2022-present tenure (coming from Nevada) continues the rebuild. Canvas Stadium (built 2017) is the modern venue identity.

## fanbase_summary

Ram fans are Fort-Collins-Ram-stalwarts. The Sonny Lubick historical anchor + Canvas Stadium dedicated-football venue + Mountain West conference identity + Rocky Mountain Showdown rivalry + Front Range geographic catchment combine into a fanbase distinct from larger flagship-state programs (Colorado, Denver-area pro sports). The Cam the Ram mascot + 'Stalwart Green' identity create unique program positioning.

## rivalry_context

Colorado (the Rocky Mountain Showdown, since 1893) is the In-State Front Range rival across MW vs Big 12 conference lines. Wyoming (Bronze Boot Trophy, since 1968) is the structural Mountain West fixture. Air Force + Boise State + Utah State are the modern Mountain West cycle annual partners. The MW realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
