## program_history

Arkansas State football's modern peak was the 2011-2015 5 consecutive Sun Belt championships dynasty (under Hugh Freeze / Gus Malzahn / Bryan Harsin / Blake Anderson). The coaching tree (Freeze to Ole Miss, Malzahn to Auburn, Harsin to Boise State) gives A-State permanent national-narrative weight. Butch Jones's 2021-present tenure continues the cycle. The 2008 mascot rename (from Indians to Red Wolves) anchored the modern identity.

## fanbase_summary

Red Wolves fans are Jonesboro-Red-Wolves-Up. The 5-title dynasty + Wolves Up identity + Sun Belt conference identity + Jonesboro Arkansas geographic catchment combine into a fanbase distinct from larger Arkansas flagship-state programs (Arkansas).

## rivalry_context

Memphis (the In-State / regional Mid-South rival) + South Alabama + Troy + Sun Belt cycle partners are the modern annual fixtures. The Sun Belt realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
