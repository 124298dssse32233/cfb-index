## program_history

Navy football's all-time peak was Wayne Hardin's 1963 9-1 + Cotton Bowl + #2 final AP rank with Roger Staubach winning the Heisman. Modern peak was Ken Niumatalolo's 2015 11-2 + Military Bowl + American Athletic Western division championship. The triple-option offense (run by Paul Johnson + Niumatalolo + Brian Newberry) is permanent program scheme identity. The 2024 9-3 under Newberry continued the competitive cycle.

## fanbase_summary

Midshipmen fans are Annapolis-Brigade-loyalists. The Brigade-of-Midshipmen + alumni network + military-family geographic dispersion create a fanbase that values service-academy mission alignment over conventional wins-only loyalty. Every senior playing their last game vs Army is the defining annual emotional arc.

## rivalry_context

Army (the Army-Navy game, since 1890) is the rivalry — one of CFB's most-storied. Air Force completes the service-academy triangle for the Commander-in-Chief's Trophy. Notre Dame (the historic non-conference rival, paused 2023, but the all-time annual count is over 90 meetings) is the lost-to-realignment fixture. The American Athletic shifts continue to reshape conference partners.
