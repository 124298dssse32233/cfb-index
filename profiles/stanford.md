## program_history

Stanford football's Jim Harbaugh era (2007-2010) rebuilt the program from the Pac-10's basement to a 12-1 BCS bowl team. David Shaw inherited and sustained through 2017 (3 Pac-12 titles, 5 NY6 bowls). The 2020s have been a flat reset. The ACC move in 2024 is a different bet entirely.

## fanbase_summary

Stanford fans are academic-loyalists. The Tree is the spiritual mascot; the band is the cultural mascot. They show up to home games in lower numbers than SEC programs but in higher percentage than the Pac-12's median. Football is one of many things Stanford does; for the fanbase that loves it, it's the one that matters most.

## rivalry_context

Cal (The Big Game, the Axe) is the historical rival. Notre Dame is the calendar feature (the longest-running intersectional series in CFB). Oregon was the modern Pac-12 rival; ACC realignment makes that lapse the saddest of the conference's many losses.
