## program_history

Oregon State football's all-time peak was Dennis Erickson's 2000 11-1 + Fiesta Bowl championship + #4 final AP rank (the program's only major bowl win). Modern peak was Jonathan Smith's 2022 10-3 + Las Vegas Bowl appearance. Smith's 2023 departure to Michigan State left Trent Bray for the 2024 Pac-12-collapse transitional season — schedules largely vs Mountain West + non-conference opponents. The Pac-12 holdout legal-entity story is the defining realignment-era moment.

## fanbase_summary

Beaver fans are Corvallis-Beaver-holdouts. The 2024 Pac-12 collapse + Oregon State's defiant continuation + Civil War rivalry preservation combine into a fanbase identity defined by survival. The 2000 Fiesta Bowl historical anchor + Erickson era's recruiting reputation + Jonathan Smith development pipeline (Chase Garbers etc) provide the historical foundation.

## rivalry_context

Oregon (the Civil War / Platypus Trophy, since 1894) is the structural in-state rival — paused 2024 due to realignment, restarted 2025+ across conference lines. Washington State (the other Pac-12 holdout) is the structural conference-survivor fixture. Mountain West fixtures (Boise State + Air Force + Fresno State) are the 2024 transitional schedule.
