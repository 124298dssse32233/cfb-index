## program_history

Charlotte football began in 2013 — one of the most-recent FBS-level program creations in CFB history. Brad Lambert (2013-2018) was the founding head coach. Will Healy's 2019 7-6 + Bahamas Bowl appearance was the first bowl appearance. Biff Poggi's 2023-present tenure continues the rebuild + the 2023 AAC entry from C-USA opened the new conference era.

## fanbase_summary

49ers fans are Charlotte-Niner-Forever-loyalists. The 2013 founding institutional memory + Jerry Richardson Stadium + Charlotte NC metropolitan-area + AAC conference identity combine into a fanbase distinct from the larger North Carolina flagship programs (NC State, UNC, Duke, Wake Forest).

## rivalry_context

ECU (the Carolina Skirmish, the in-state cross-conference rival) is the structural North Carolina fixture. UTSA + Memphis + Tulane + the AAC cycle partners are the modern annual fixtures. The American Athletic realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
