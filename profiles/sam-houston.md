## program_history

Sam Houston football's all-time peak was K.C. Keeler's 2020 FCS national championship (played in spring 2021 due to COVID). Modern peak is the 2023 FBS reclassification + Conference USA entry + 2024 8-4 second FBS season — the most-recent program to make the FCS-to-FBS-conference-relevance ascent. Keeler's tenure (2014-present) spans the FCS championship + FBS transition + sustained competitive cycles. The transition is the defining recent CFB ascent narrative.

## fanbase_summary

Bearkat fans are Huntsville-Bearkat-loyalists. The K.C. Keeler tenure + 2020 FCS championship + 2023 FBS transition + Huntsville TX geographic catchment + Texas State University rivalry (the in-conference newcomer pairing) combine into a fanbase that values rapid ascent + national legitimacy. The 2024 8-4 second-season + bowl eligibility confirmed the program's competitive viability at FBS tier.

## rivalry_context

Stephen F. Austin (the historic FCS-era rival) is the structural Texas fixture. Jacksonville State (the FBS-newcomer cohort partner) is the recurring annual cycle. The 2023 Conference USA entry brought WKU + Liberty + UTEP + others as new annual partners. The C-USA realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
