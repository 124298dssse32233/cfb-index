## program_history

James Madison football's modern era spans three FCS national championships (Mickey Matthews 2004, Mike Houston 2016, plus multiple other title-game appearances), the 2022 FBS reclassification (transition to Sun Belt), and the 2023 11-2 Sun Belt East championship game appearance under Curt Cignetti. Cignetti's 2024 departure to Indiana (producing the Indiana 11-1 + CFP appearance) is one of the more-improbable cross-program coaching arcs in CFB. Bob Chesney's 2024 tenure continues the Sun Belt era.

## fanbase_summary

Duke fans are Harrisonburg-Duke-loyalists. The Shenandoah Valley geography + three FCS national championships + 2022 FBS reclassification + Bridgeforth Stadium purple-and-gold identity + Curt Cignetti coaching-tree narrative combine into a fanbase that values rapid ascent + national legitimacy.

## rivalry_context

App State (the Sun Belt era structural rival) is the modern annual cycle game. Liberty (the in-Virginia regional rival) is the recurring annual cycle. The 2022 Sun Belt entry brought Marshall + Coastal Carolina + Georgia Southern + others as new annual partners. The Sun Belt realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
