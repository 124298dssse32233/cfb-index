## program_history

Oklahoma State football's modern peak was 2011 — 12-1, Big 12 champions, Fiesta Bowl winners, five spots from a national title under Mike Gundy. The Gundy era (2005-2024) is the longest-tenured Power 5 head-coach run in CFB, producing 9+ wins in 14 of 20 seasons. Boone Pickens' $500M+ in athletic-department donations rebuilt the stadium and competitive infrastructure. The 2024 Bedlam pause + SEC realignment chaos is the next defining era.

## fanbase_summary

Cowboy fans are Stillwater-loyalist-cowboy. The orange-out at Boone Pickens Stadium, the Pistol Pete identification, the patience-with-Gundy through every controversy. Saturday tailgates in Stillwater (population 50K) are a destination for the diaspora.

## rivalry_context

Oklahoma (Bedlam) is the defining rivalry — 120+ years, the in-state stakes, the SEC departure that paused the series in 2024. Texas (the Big 12 ECC rival) was the second-tier game. The 2024 conference reshuffling reset every meaningful OK State rivalry context.
