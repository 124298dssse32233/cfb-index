## program_history

Tulane football's modern peak was Willie Fritz's 2022 12-2 + American Athletic championship + Cotton Bowl championship + #9 final AP rank. All-time peak was Ted Cox's 1934 unbeaten + Rose Bowl appearance. The 1925 Sugar Bowl founding is permanent institutional heritage. Fritz's 2024 departure to Houston opened Jon Sumrall's tenure. The Yulman Stadium 2014 construction (Tulane's first on-campus stadium since 1974) anchored the modern era.

## fanbase_summary

Green Wave fans are New-Orleans-Roll-Wave-loyalists. The Sugar Bowl founding heritage + Yulman Stadium on-campus + Uptown New Orleans setting + Mardi Gras game-day proximity + 2022 Cotton Bowl run create a fanbase identity rooted in southern-football tradition + New Orleans cultural identity.

## rivalry_context

LSU (the In-State Battle for the Rag, a historic rivalry since 1893, on hiatus since 1994) is the structural Louisiana fixture, awaiting potential revival. Memphis (the AAC era rival) is the structural conference fixture. The American Athletic realignment continues to develop annual rival cycles.
