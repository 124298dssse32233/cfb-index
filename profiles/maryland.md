## program_history

Maryland football's 1953 national title is the program's only major peak; the 2001 Orange Bowl trip under Ralph Friedgen is the closest modern equivalent. The 2014 Big Ten move was the strategic pivot — escape ACC mediocrity, embrace Big Ten weight-class. The post-move record is 6-6 to 8-4 with the occasional 9-win year.

## fanbase_summary

Terps fans are DC-mid-Atlantic-loyalists. Capital-region geography means football competes with NFL Sundays and basketball Tuesdays. The uniforms are the spiritual identity; the basketball program is the cultural anchor; football is the patient project. SECU Stadium tailgates skew younger and more cosmopolitan than Big Ten standard.

## rivalry_context

West Virginia (the historical regional rival) was lost to Big 12 realignment. Penn State is the Big Ten East structural blocker. Rutgers is the conference-move-twin from 2014. The post-Big Ten realignment of the 2020s reshuffled every meaningful rivalry context.
