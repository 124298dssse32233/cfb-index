## program_history

Troy football's modern peaks were Neal Brown's 2017 11-2 + Sun Belt championship + Dollar General Bowl + LSU upset (24-21) and Jon Sumrall's 2022 12-2 + New Mexico Bowl championship. The 2001 FBS reclassification opened the modern era. Multiple Sun Belt championships across Brown + Sumrall + Larry Blakeney + Chip Lindsey + Gerad Parker eras. Sumrall's 2024 departure to Tulane opened Gerad Parker's 2024-present tenure.

## fanbase_summary

Trojan fans are Troy-Trojan-Bleed-Cardinal. The Alabama Sun-Belt-shadow identity + FBS reclassification ascent + 2017 LSU upset + 2022 Sumrall 12-win season + cardinal-and-black colors combine into a fanbase distinct from the three larger Alabama programs (Bama, Auburn, UAB).

## rivalry_context

South Alabama (the Sun Belt structural rival, Battle for the Belt of the South) is the In-Alabama fixture. App State + Coastal Carolina + Marshall are the Sun Belt cycle annual partners. The Sun Belt realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
