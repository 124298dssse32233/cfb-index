## program_history

Baylor football's modern peak was Art Briles' 2013-2015 Big 12 dominance — back-to-back conference titles, top-5 finishes, RG3's 2011 Heisman cementing the offensive blueprint. The 2016 Briles departure (amid scandal) was the structural reset; Matt Rhule's 2017-2019 rebuild produced the 2019 Sugar Bowl team; Dave Aranda's 2021 Big 12 title + Sugar Bowl proved the program could win without Briles.

## fanbase_summary

Bear fans are Waco-faith-loyalists. McLane Stadium tailgates incorporate faith-community elements (pre-game blessings, post-game prayer circles) more visibly than any P5 program. The 2010s Briles era brought a national-recruiting visibility the post-scandal years had to rebuild from scratch.

## rivalry_context

TCU (the Iron Skillet's lesser cousin, the Bluebonnet Rivalry) is the in-state intra-conference structural rival. Texas Tech (post-realignment) is the second-tier. The 2024 Big 12 realignment (Texas/Oklahoma exits) reshaped Baylor's competitive landscape — now a senior Big 12 power program.
