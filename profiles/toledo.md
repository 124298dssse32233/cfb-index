## program_history

Toledo football's all-time peak was Frank Lauterbur's 1969-1972 tenure — particularly the 35-game unbeaten regular-season streak (national record at the time) + three MAC championships in four years. Modern peak is Jason Candle's 2016-present tenure (9+ seasons, multiple MAC championship game appearances). Matt Campbell's 2011-2015 era was the bridge between Schiano-era and Candle-era. The Glass Bowl (built 1937) is permanent program venue.

## fanbase_summary

Rocket fans are Toledo-Glass-Bowl-Rocket-loyalists. The Glass Bowl WPA-era architecture + 1969-1972 unbeaten-streak institutional memory + Jason Candle longevity + Toledo glass-industry historical economic identity combine into a fanbase distinct from larger flagship-state programs. The MAC conference identity + sustained competitive cycles anchor program positioning.

## rivalry_context

Bowling Green (the Battle of I-75, since 1919) is the In-State MAC rival. NIU + Western Michigan + Eastern Michigan + Central Michigan are the modern MAC West cycle annual partners. The MAC realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
