## program_history

UTEP football's modern peak was Mike Price's 2004-2012 tenure — particularly the 2005 8-4 + Houston Bowl appearance. Price's pass-offense identity + the recruiting reach into Texas + the post-Alabama redemption arc made the era distinctive. The program's longest-standing identity is the Sun Bowl Stadium dual-use heritage (built 1963, hosts annual Sun Bowl since 1936 at this site). Scotty Walden's 2024-present tenure continues the cycle.

## fanbase_summary

Miner fans are El-Paso-Miner-Picks-Up. The El Paso borderland geographic catchment + mining-heritage Picks Up identity + Sun Bowl Stadium dual-use venue + Franklin Mountains backdrop + Conference USA conference identity combine into a fanbase distinct from larger Texas flagship-state programs.

## rivalry_context

New Mexico State (the Battle of I-10, since 1914) is the structural in-region rival. Texas (the historic Texas border-region rival) is the lost-to-realignment fixture. Conference USA cycle partners (WKU + Liberty + Jacksonville State + others) are the modern annual fixtures. The C-USA realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
