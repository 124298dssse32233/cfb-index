## program_history

Wake Forest football's peak was Jim Grobe's 2006-2008 era — 2006 ACC championship, 2007 Orange Bowl appearance, top-15 finishes. The Grobe approach (player development, redshirting, low-recruiting-ranking but high-ceiling QB pipeline) produced the only ACC title for a sub-10K-enrollment program in the BCS era. Dave Clawson's 2014-present tenure continues the development-first model.

## fanbase_summary

Demon Deacon fans are Winston-Salem-brand-loyalists. Wake's enrollment of ~5,400 undergrads is the smallest in the ACC; the fanbase concentrates regional pride in a way that compensates for raw numbers. Old-gold-and-black tailgates skew academic + alumni-network demographics.

## rivalry_context

NC State (annual ACC fixture) is the secondary rivalry. UNC (the three-way Tobacco Road football scrimmage) is the cultural-overlap rival. Notre Dame (occasional ACC partner) and Clemson (ACC division blocker) are the modern competitive structural barriers.
