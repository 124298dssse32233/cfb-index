## program_history

Ole Miss football is the program that built The Grove tailgate into one of CFB's three best Saturday experiences without consistently winning enough games to justify it. Hugh Freeze's 2014-2015 run produced a #1 ranking and back-to-back upset wins over Alabama. Lane Kiffin's 2020-present tenure has stabilized at top-25 with a 2024 SEC West breakout.

## fanbase_summary

Rebel fans are aristocratic-Southern-loyalists. The Grove is the spiritual center; Hotty Toddy is the linguistic identity; the Walk of Champions is the program ritual. Patient with the football product because the tailgate alone justifies the trip.

## rivalry_context

Mississippi State (Egg Bowl) is the calendar gravitational center — Thanksgiving week, state-pride stakes, the rivalry that decides offseason talk-radio for both sides. Alabama is the SEC West structural blocker. LSU is the geographic rival the SEC East-West realignment muddled.
