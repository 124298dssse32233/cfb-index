## program_history

Southern Miss football's modern peak was Larry Fedora's 2011 12-2 + Conference USA championship + Hawaii Bowl championship + #19 final AP rank. The 1988-2010 21-consecutive-winning-seasons streak crossed Curley Hallman + Jeff Bower + Larry Fedora eras + spanned the Mississippi G5 ceiling. Brett Favre's 1987-1990 QB development era + 1991 NFL draft pick + Hall of Fame career give the program retroactive elevation. Will Hall's 2021-present tenure continues the rebuild.

## fanbase_summary

Golden Eagle fans are Hattiesburg-Eagle-SMTTT-loyalists ('Southern Miss to the Top'). The 21-winning-seasons streak institutional memory + Brett Favre narrative + M.M. Roberts Stadium 'The Rock' venue + Sun Belt conference identity + Hattiesburg Pine Belt geographic catchment combine into a fanbase distinct from the larger Mississippi flagship-state programs (Ole Miss, Mississippi State).

## rivalry_context

Memphis (the Battle for the Brass Spittoon, since 1936) is the long-running annual rival. Tulane (the Battle for the Bell, since 1935) is the structural historic fixture. Sun Belt cycle partners (Marshall + App State + Coastal Carolina) are the modern annual fixtures. The Sun Belt realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
