## program_history

UTSA football began in 2011 + reached the FBS in 2012, Conference USA in 2013, American Athletic in 2023. The modern peak was Jeff Traylor's 2021 12-2 + Conference USA championship + Frisco Bowl + #24 final AP rank. The 2022 Conference USA championship + 2023 AAC entry continued the trajectory. Traylor's 2020-present tenure is the longest sustained head-coach run in program history.

## fanbase_summary

Roadrunner fans are San-Antonio-Roadrunner-Birds-Up. The Alamodome NFL-grade venue + downtown San Antonio setting + Birds Up identity + Conference USA-to-AAC ascent + Jeff Traylor narrative combine into a fanbase that values rapid ascent + Texas-football regional identity.

## rivalry_context

Texas State (the Battle for Interstate 35) is the In-Texas Sun Belt cross-conference rival. UTEP + Rice + North Texas + the AAC cycle partners (East Carolina + Tulane + Memphis + Tulsa) are the modern annual fixtures. The American Athletic realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
