## program_history

Georgia State football began in 2010 under founding head coach Bill Curry. The program went FCS 2010 → FBS 2013 → C-USA 2014 → Sun Belt 2014. Shawn Elliott's 2017-present tenure produced the program's modern peak (2017 Cure Bowl championship, his first year). The 2017 Center Parc Stadium opening (converted Turner Field) modernized the venue.

## fanbase_summary

Panther fans are Atlanta-Panther-Light-It-Up. The 2010 founding institutional memory + Center Parc Stadium's MLB-to-CFB heritage + Atlanta downtown setting + Shawn Elliott narrative + Sun Belt conference identity combine into a fanbase distinct from larger Georgia flagship programs (Georgia, Georgia Tech, Georgia Southern).

## rivalry_context

Georgia Southern (the Modern Day Hate, since 2014) is the In-State Sun Belt structural rival. App State + Coastal Carolina + Marshall + Troy are the Sun Belt cycle annual partners. The Sun Belt realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
