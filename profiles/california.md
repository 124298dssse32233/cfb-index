## program_history

California football's modern peak was Jeff Tedford's 2004 10-2 + Holiday Bowl championship + #4 final AP rank (Aaron Rodgers as QB before the 2005 NFL draft). All-time canon moment is 'The Play' from the 1982 Big Game (5 laterals + Stanford band on the field). Joe Roth's terminally-ill-but-still-playing 1976 season + the retired #12 jersey is the program's emotional anchor. The 2024 ACC entry from Pac-12 collapse opened the new conference era under Justin Wilcox.

## fanbase_summary

Golden Bear fans are Berkeley-Old-Blues-loyalists. The academic-elite + Pac-12-historical + Bay Area + Berkeley campus combine into a fanbase identity that values heritage equal to wins. The Stanford rivalry (the Big Game, since 1892) is the annual cultural anchor. The 2024 ACC realignment forced a fanbase identity reset that's still developing.

## rivalry_context

Stanford (the Big Game, since 1892, the Stanford Axe trophy) is the structural rival. UCLA / USC (Pac-12 era fixtures) were lost to 2024 SEC / Big Ten realignment. Oregon (Pac-12 historical) is the lost-to-Big-Ten fixture. The 2024 ACC cycle brought Virginia + Virginia Tech + Wake Forest as new annual partners — the most-geographically-disconnected ACC fit in the conference.
