## program_history

Florida State football is the dynasty CFB's ACC era was built around. Bobby Bowden's 1976-2009 tenure produced 2 national titles (1993, 1999), 14 straight top-5 finishes (1987-2000 — still the all-time record), and the cultural template every ACC program tried to copy. The post-Bowden era is a 15-year search for the next dynastic arc.

## fanbase_summary

FSU fans are heritage-loyalists. They remember the dynasty in detail, hold every successor coach to that standard, and treat Doak Campbell as a sacred site. The 2013 title was the brief return to that altitude; the 2024 ACC unpleasantness reset the clock.

## rivalry_context

Miami is the historical national-stakes rival (the 1980s-90s Wide Right / Wide Left era). Florida is the in-state regular-season finale that defines the calendar. Clemson is the ACC era replacement for both.
