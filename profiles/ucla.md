## program_history

UCLA football's modern peak was the Terry Donahue era (1976-1995) — 5 Pac-10 titles, the 1990s Cotton Bowl run. Karl Dorrell, Rick Neuheisel, and Jim Mora were the wilderness era; Chip Kelly's 2018-2023 tenure produced moments but no ceiling. DeShaun Foster's 2024 hire is the next bet, with the Big Ten realignment changing the entire structural context.

## fanbase_summary

Bruin fans are sun-belt-patient-loyalists. They tailgate the Rose Bowl parking lot with the LA car-culture sprawl that no other program can replicate. The Eight Clap is the spiritual marker; the basketball program's eight national championships are the cultural anchor; football is real and patient.

## rivalry_context

USC (Crosstown Cup, sometimes Victory Bell) is the structural rival — same conference now (Big Ten as of 2024), same city, same recruiting territory. Cal is the historical Pac-10 rival the realignment dismantled. The Big Ten realignment makes Wisconsin, Iowa, and Michigan the new annual cycle.
