## program_history

Ohio football's modern peak was Frank Solich's 2005-2020 tenure — 115 total wins, multiple MAC championship game appearances, multiple bowl wins (including the 2011 Famous Idaho Potato Bowl championship). All-time peak was Bill Hess's 1968 10-1 + Tangerine Bowl championship. Tim Albin's 2021-present tenure continues the Solich-era trajectory. The Peden Stadium (built 1929) hillside venue is permanent program identity.

## fanbase_summary

Bobcat fans are Athens-OH-Bobcat-loyalists. The southeast-Ohio Appalachian Foothills geography + Peden Stadium hillside venue + Frank Solich era institutional memory + Battle of the Bricks rivalry + 'OU Oh Yeah!' chant combine into a fanbase that values heritage equal to wins.

## rivalry_context

Miami (OH) (Battle of the Bricks, since 1908) is the In-State MAC rival. Bowling Green + Toledo + NIU + Western Michigan are the MAC West cycle annual partners. Marshall (the historic regional rival, now Sun Belt) is the lost-to-realignment fixture. The MAC realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
