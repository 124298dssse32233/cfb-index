## program_history

Houston football's modern eras run across three peaks — Case Keenum's 2011 13-1 + C-USA championship + NCAA passing yards record, Tom Herman's 2015 13-1 + AAC championship + Peach Bowl win, and the 2023 Big 12 conference entry. The Air Raid offense identity (from June Jones + Kevin Sumlin + Tom Herman eras) anchors the program's modern signature. Dana Holgorsen's 2019-2023 tenure was the bridge era between AAC and Big 12.

## fanbase_summary

Cougar fans are Houston-Cougar-Paw-loyalists. The Houston metropolitan area's CFB landscape (vs Texas / Texas A&M for primacy) makes the program's identity defiance-rooted. The Cougar Paw + Air Raid offensive identity + 2015 Peach Bowl win created a fanbase identity that survived the G5 → P5 transition.

## rivalry_context

Texas A&M (the historic Bayou Bucket / Cougar Paw rivalry) is the structural Houston-area fixture. Rice (the in-city rival, 110+ meetings) is the cross-city annual game. The 2023 Big 12 entry brought TCU + Baylor + Texas Tech as new conference fixtures. The Texas rivalry was lost to SEC realignment but the Big 12 cycle continues to develop.
