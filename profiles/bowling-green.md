## program_history

Bowling Green football's all-time peak was Doyt Perry's 1959 9-0 + #20 final AP rank. The 1955-1964 Perry era + multiple MAC championships made BG a regional powerhouse. Modern peak was Urban Meyer's 2001-2002 tenure (his first head-coach job, consecutive 8-3 + 9-3 + bowl appearances). Meyer's BG launchpad + subsequent Utah + Florida + Ohio State + retirement career gives BG permanent national-narrative weight. Scot Loeffler's 2019-present tenure continues the cycle.

## fanbase_summary

Falcon fans are BG-Falcon-Ziggy-Zoomba-loyalists. The Doyt Perry historical anchor + Urban Meyer launchpad narrative + 'Ay Ziggy Zoomba' fight song + MAC conference identity + Bowling Green Ohio geographic catchment combine into a fanbase distinct from larger Ohio flagship-state programs (Ohio State).

## rivalry_context

Toledo (the Battle of I-75, since 1919) is the In-State MAC rival. Ohio (the Battle of the Bricks era partner) is the modern MAC cycle annual fixture. Eastern Michigan + Western Michigan + Northern Illinois are the MAC West cycle annual partners. The MAC realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
