## program_history

UCF football's modern eras span Scott Frost's 2017 13-0 + Peach Bowl + self-declared national championship, Josh Heupel's 2018 12-1 + Fiesta Bowl championship, and Gus Malzahn's 2023 Big 12 entry from American Athletic. The 2017 defiance — banners + commemorative rings + national-champion merchandise — became permanent program identity. The 2018 G5-CFP injustice argument continued to drive narrative. The 2023 Big 12 entry completed the G5-to-P5 ascent.

## fanbase_summary

Knight fans are Orlando-Charge-On-loyalists. The 2017 self-declared national championship + 2018 Fiesta Bowl + 2023 Big 12 entry create a multi-era fanbase identity built around defiance + ascent. The Bounce House venue + Charge On phrase + black-and-gold colors anchor every game-day visual.

## rivalry_context

USF (the in-state War on I-4 rivalry) is the structural Florida fixture. UConn (the American Athletic era rival) is the lost-to-realignment fixture. The 2023 Big 12 entry brought Texas Tech + TCU + Baylor as new annual partners. The Florida State / Miami / Florida fixtures remain wishlist non-conference targets.
