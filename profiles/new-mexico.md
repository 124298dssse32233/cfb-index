## program_history

New Mexico football's modern peak was Rocky Long's 2007 9-4 + New Mexico Bowl championship (his first year as head coach). Long's 2009-2018 tenure produced the 3-3-5 defensive scheme that influenced coaching trees nationally. All-time peak was Dennis Franchione's 1997 9-4 + Insight Bowl appearance. Bob Davie's 2012-2019 tenure was the bridge era. Jason Eck's 2025-present tenure is the current cycle. The University Stadium (built 1960) altitude is permanent venue identity.

## fanbase_summary

Lobo fans are Albuquerque-Lobo-pack-loyalists. The University Stadium 5,300 ft altitude + Lobo wolf-pack identity + Rocky Long 3-3-5 defensive legacy + Albuquerque desert geographic catchment combine into a fanbase distinct from larger flagship-state programs. The Mountain West conference identity + sustained competitive cycles anchor program positioning.

## rivalry_context

New Mexico State (the Rio Grande Rivalry, since 1894 — one of CFB's oldest rivalries) is the In-State structural rival, paused with conference-tier divergence in recent years. Air Force + Boise State + Fresno State + UNLV are the Mountain West cycle annual partners. The MW realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
