## program_history

Western Kentucky football's modern peak was Jeff Brohm's 2014-2016 tenure — particularly the 2015 12-2 + Conference USA championship + Boca Raton Bowl win + 2016 11-3 + Boca Raton Bowl win. All-time peak is Jack Harbaugh's 2002 FCS national championship. The 2007 FBS reclassification (from FCS Division I-AA) opened the modern era; 2008-2013 was the transitional struggle period. Brohm's 2017 departure to Purdue + Mike Sanford Jr's 2017-2018 era + the Tyson Helton + Mike Sanford eras continued the cycle.

## fanbase_summary

Hilltopper fans are Bowling-Green-KY-Hilltopper-Nation. The 2002 FCS championship + Brohm Air Raid era + Big Red mascot iconography + Conference USA conference identity + the Bowling Green KY geographic catchment (not to be confused with Bowling Green OH, the BGSU MAC program) combine into a fanbase distinct from larger flagship-state programs.

## rivalry_context

Middle Tennessee (the 100 Miles of Hate, since 1936) is the structural Conference USA rival. UAB (the Conference USA cycle partner) is the recurring annual game. Marshall (the Conference USA era rival, now Sun Belt) is the lost-to-realignment fixture. The C-USA realignment continues to develop annual rival cycles.
