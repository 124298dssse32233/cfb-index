## program_history

Missouri football's modern peak is Gary Pinkel's 2013-2014 era — back-to-back SEC East titles, Cotton Bowl win, top-5 finishes. Pinkel's 14-year tenure (2001-2015) built the program from Big 12 doormat into SEC competitor. The post-Pinkel Barry Odom and Eli Drinkwitz eras stabilized at SEC top-25 cycles; the 2023 11-2 and 2024 10-3 seasons under Drinkwitz are the latest peaks.

## fanbase_summary

Tiger fans are show-me-state-patient-loyalists. The M-I-Z chant carries diaspora identity to road games more than any other SEC newcomer's identity marker; the Mizzou Mafia recruiting network demonstrates fan-organized influence. Faurot Field tailgates skew working-class Missouri by demographic.

## rivalry_context

Kansas (Border War) was the historic Big 12-era rival the SEC move dismantled — restored as a non-conference fixture in the 2020s. Arkansas (Battle Line Rivalry) is the SEC-era replacement. Florida and Georgia are the SEC East structural blockers. Realignment continues to reshape every meaningful Mizzou rivalry.
