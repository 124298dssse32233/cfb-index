## program_history

Liberty football's modern eras span Hugh Freeze's 2019-2022 rebuild (four straight bowl appearances + 30+ wins + 2020 #17 AP rank) and Jamey Chadwell's 2023 13-1 + Conference USA championship + Fiesta Bowl appearance + #18 final AP rank. The program reclassified from FCS to FBS in 2018, played as FBS independent 2018-2022, and entered Conference USA in 2023. The Fiesta Bowl was the first ever New Year's Six bowl appearance by a Conference USA team.

## fanbase_summary

Flame fans are Lynchburg-Flames-loyalists. The FCS-to-FBS-conference ascent + Hugh Freeze rebuild + Jamey Chadwell 2023 peak + religious-university institutional identity combine into a fanbase that values rapid ascent + national legitimacy. Williams Stadium's mid-size capacity + Lynchburg geography make the home-game experience distinct from larger flagship programs.

## rivalry_context

Old Dominion (the in-Virginia regional rival) is a Conference USA / Sun Belt era fixture. Coastal Carolina (the Jamey Chadwell continuity rival, where Chadwell built his prior reputation) is a recurring cycle. The 2023 Conference USA entry brought UTEP + WKU + others as new annual partners. The C-USA realignment continues to develop.
