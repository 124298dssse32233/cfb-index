## program_history

San José State football's modern peak was Brent Brennan's 2020 7-1 + Mountain West championship + Arizona Bowl championship + #25 final AP (during the COVID-truncated season). All-time peak was Terry Shea's 1990 9-2-1 + California Bowl championship. The Silicon Valley identity + 1893 institutional founding + Mountain West conference identity anchor the program. Ken Niumatalolo's 2024-present tenure (coming from Navy) continues the cycle.

## fanbase_summary

Spartan fans are San-Jose-Spartan-All-Spartan-loyalists. The Silicon Valley setting + 2020 COVID-era championship narrative + CEFCU Stadium venue + Mountain West conference identity + Bay Area G5 alternative + Spartan mascot heritage combine into a fanbase distinct from larger Bay Area programs (Stanford, Cal).

## rivalry_context

Fresno State (the Battle of the Valleys) + UNLV + Boise State + San Diego State + the Mountain West cycle partners are the modern annual fixtures. The Mountain West realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
