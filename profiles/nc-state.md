## program_history

NC State football's modern identity is the QB pipeline — Philip Rivers (1999-2003), Russell Wilson (2008-2010), Mike Glennon (2009-2012), Bradley Chubb / Ryan Finley (2014-2018), Devin Leary (2020-2022). Dave Doeren's 2013-present tenure built sustained ACC competitiveness; the 2010s-2020s arc produced multiple 8-9 win seasons + bowl wins.

## fanbase_summary

Wolfpack fans are Raleigh-loud-loyalists. The howl tradition is the program's defining auditory identity; Carter-Finley Stadium tailgates with the red-out unify the fanbase. NC State's agricultural-school heritage shapes the working-class identity that contrasts with UNC's academic prestige.

## rivalry_context

North Carolina (the Old North State Rivalry) is the structural in-state ACC rival — academic-prestige UNC vs agricultural NC State. East Carolina (the State Border War) is the secondary. Wake Forest is the three-way Tobacco Road football scrimmage. The 2024 realignment continues to reshape ACC dynamics.
