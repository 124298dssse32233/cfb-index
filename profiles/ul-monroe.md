## program_history

ULM football's notable narratives span the 1987 NCAA Division I-AA national championship (then Northeast Louisiana), the 2007 program rename, and Todd Berry's 2012 8-5 + Independence Bowl appearance + the iconic Arkansas upset (34-31 OT). Bryant Vincent's 2024-present tenure continues the cycle. The Sun Belt conference identity + Monroe North Louisiana geographic catchment anchor positioning.

## fanbase_summary

Warhawk fans are Monroe-Warhawk-Fly-High-loyalists. The 1987 FCS championship + 2012 Arkansas upset + 2007 rename institutional memory + Malone Stadium + Sun Belt conference identity combine into a fanbase distinct from larger Louisiana flagship programs.

## rivalry_context

Louisiana (the In-State Battle on the Bayou) is the Sun Belt structural rival. Arkansas State + South Alabama + Sun Belt cycle partners are the modern annual fixtures. The Sun Belt realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
