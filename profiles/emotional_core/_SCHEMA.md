# Emotional-Core sidecar — schema

Per-program `emotional_core/<slug>.yaml` sidecars. The structured "how this fanbase feels"
layer that makes the Team Story Card bespoke. Spec: [docs/design-system/63-per-program-emotional-identity.md](../../docs/design-system/63-per-program-emotional-identity.md).

**Why a sidecar (not the profile):** authored safely without touching the live-read `profiles/*.md`.
A future renderer merges the sidecar over the profile. May be folded into the profile later — the
sidecar is the *authoring surface*.

## Lifecycle
`status: todo → drafted → authored → reviewed → live`
- **todo** — auto-seeded scaffold (DRAFT fields from the profile, TODO soul blank). Generated by `scripts/seed_emotional_cores.py`.
- **drafted** — a first pass exists, rough.
- **authored** — the soul is filled WITH the owner; passes the validator's structural + enum + never_use checks.
- **reviewed** — the owner signed off (esp. the sensitive wound/delusion fields).
- **live** — wired to the crown.

Run `python scripts/verify_emotional_cores.py --strict` before flipping to `reviewed`/`live`.

## Fields (enums)
- `emotional_core.primary` / `.secondary` — `standard | grief | grievance | restoration | redemption | disbelief | spectacle | delusion | deprival | contentment | house-money | honor`
- `emotional_core.one_line` — **the soul**: the single sentence the page leads from.
- `self_image.archetype` — `blue-blood-living-up | blue-blood-fallen | riser-proving | sleeping-giant | perennial-almost | lovable-have-not | orphaned`
- `the_wound.handling` — `referenceable | never-a-punchline | dated-grievance` (binding safety rule)
- `the_wound.freshness` — `this-era | generational | all-time`
- `the_delusion.tone` — `earnest | self-aware | load-bearing | none` (affectionate, NEVER mocking)
- `the_grudge_texture.texture` — `blood-feud | championship | little-brother | killed-by-realignment | border-war`
- `the_coach_relationship` — `savior | legends-shadow | forever-ours | hot-seat-churn | total-trust`
- `the_chip.villain` — `committee | national_media | blue_bloods | big_brother | none`
- `register` — free text (seeded from the profile `voice_register`).
- `never_use` — LAW. Seeded from the profile; the validator hard-fails any authored line that violates it.

## Handling rules (from doc 63 §8)
- The wound is sacred — a `never-a-punchline` wound is referenced with gravity or not at all.
- The delusion is affectionate, never mocking — compile the fanbase's own self-aware faith, attributed.
- `never_use` is law; attribution always; the core is the *fanbase's* feeling, never the site's opinion.
- Cores shift — re-derive `primary` when the program crosses a threshold (a drought breaks, a snub lands).

## Files in this directory
- `<slug>.yaml` — one per FBS team (the canvas).
- `_STATUS.md` — the team-by-team progress board (regenerated by the seeder).
- `_SCHEMA.md` — this file.

## core_variant (optional finer cohort — added 2026-06-14)
Splits the two over-loaded cores into smaller sub-cohorts (restoration=35, grievance=22 were ~45% of FBS).
Lives under `emotional_core.core_variant`. Optional; validated only when present.
- `restoration` → `dynasty-restoration` (a fallen champion climbing back) | `perpetual-rebuild` (mid-tier churning back) | `regime-reset` (lost the coach who built it, restarting) | `first-ascension` (building a FIRST height — candidate for a primary re-tag to disbelief/house-money)
- `grievance` → `the-snub` (a specific dated injustice) | `the-ceiling` (G5/class structural chip) | `the-chronic-chip` (a program that fights and never gets the respect/title)
The operative page-design cohort is `primary × self_image.archetype × core_variant` (multi-axis, per design-system 71) — not the primary alone.
