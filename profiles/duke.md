## program_history

Duke football's modern peak was Mike Elko's 2022-2023 era — back-to-back nine-win seasons, the first sustained competitive stretch in over a decade. David Cutcliffe's 2013-2014 Coastal Division titles preceded the Elko reset. The 1942 wartime Rose Bowl is the program's only national-stage relic. Manny Diaz's 2024 transition is the latest era.

## fanbase_summary

Blue Devil fans are Durham-academic-loyalists. Football fanbase is smaller and more intermittent than basketball; Wallace Wade Stadium tailgates skew quieter than Big 12/SEC norms. The 2022-2024 Elko era brought competitive Saturdays back to Durham; sustaining requires Manny Diaz's continuity.

## rivalry_context

North Carolina (Tobacco Road football rivalry, "the Victory Bell") is the structural in-state ACC rival. NC State and Wake Forest are the in-state ACC three-way structural cohort. Notre Dame (occasional, "the Holy Wars" 1957 game) is the historic blue-blood relic.
