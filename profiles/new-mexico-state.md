## program_history

New Mexico State football's modern peak was Jerry Kill's 2023 10-5 + Conference USA championship game appearance + Quick Lane Bowl championship — breaking decades of bowl-game droughts (the previous bowl was the 1960 Sun Bowl championship under Warren Woodson). The 2023 Conference USA entry opened the modern era after years of FBS independence. Tony Sanchez's 2024-present tenure continues the cycle.

## fanbase_summary

Aggie fans are Las-Cruces-Aggie-borderland-loyalists. The 1888 founding + 1960 Sun Bowl historical anchor + Jerry Kill 2023 breakthrough + Aggie Memorial Stadium + C-USA conference identity + Las Cruces borderland geographic catchment combine into a fanbase distinct from larger Southwest programs.

## rivalry_context

New Mexico (the Rio Grande Rivalry, since 1894 — one of CFB's oldest rivalries) is the In-State structural rival across conference-tier divergence. UTEP (the Battle of I-10, since 1914) is the structural in-region rival. C-USA cycle partners (WKU + Liberty + Jacksonville State) are the modern annual fixtures.
