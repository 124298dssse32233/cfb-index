## program_history

App State football's all-time canon moment is the 2007 Michigan upset (34-32 in the Big House, FCS App State over #5 FBS Michigan). The all-time peak was Jerry Moore's 2005-2007 three consecutive FCS national championships (only the second program ever). The 2014 FBS reclassification opened the modern era; the 2018 Sun Belt championship + sustained competitive cycles + 2025 Sun Belt East title chase continued the ascent. Sun Belt rivalries (Coastal Carolina + Marshall + Georgia Southern) anchor the modern conference identity.

## fanbase_summary

Mountaineer fans are Boone-mountain-Mountaineers. The mountain-town geography + Take the Stairs mantra + 2007 Michigan upset canon + sustained competitive identity combine into a fanbase distinct from the larger flagship-state programs. The Yosef costume + black-and-gold colors + Kidd Brewer Stadium altitude anchor every game-day visual.

## rivalry_context

Marshall (the In-State Mountain rival from the FCS / Sun Belt cycle) is the structural conference fixture. Coastal Carolina (the Sun Belt era newer rival) is the modern annual cycle game. Georgia Southern (the FCS / Sun Belt historical rival) is the structural fixture. The Sun Belt realignment continues to develop new rival cycles.
