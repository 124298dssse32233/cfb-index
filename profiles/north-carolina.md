## program_history

North Carolina football's modern peak is Mack Brown's first tenure (1988-1997) — building the program from doormat into 10-win consistency, the Bob Crawford / Curtis Whitley era teams. The 1980 Lawrence Taylor-Kelvin Bryant 11-1 season is the program's only top-10 AP finish in the post-WW2 era. Mack Brown's 2019 return is the modern reset; Sam Howell QB era (2019-2021), conference-rebuilding seasons, ACC competitiveness against the FSU-Clemson tier.

## fanbase_summary

Tar Heel fans are basketball-first-football-curious. Kenan Memorial Stadium tailgates are smaller than the Dean Smith Center basketball atmosphere; the football fanbase's loyalty is patient but limited by the basketball program's eight national championships and 100+ AP top-10 seasons.

## rivalry_context

NC State (Carolina-State rivalry, "the Old North State Rivalry") is the structural in-state rival. Duke (the Victory Bell) is the Tobacco Road rivalry where football meets basketball culture. Virginia (the South's Oldest Rivalry, started 1892) is the longest-running. The post-realignment 2020s ACC chaos reshuffles everything.
