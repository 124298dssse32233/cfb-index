## Identity
Conference USA is the proving ground where mid-major coaches build their résumés and quarterbacks put up video game numbers nobody in the Power Four wants to acknowledge. It's been picked apart by the Sun Belt, the AAC, and realignment vultures for a decade, and it still lines up every Saturday. Sam Houston, Jacksonville State, New Mexico State — the new blood keeps the heartbeat going even as the old guard gets poached.

## Voice
Fans inside C-USA talk in defiant shorthand — they know their program isn't on ESPN's radar and they genuinely don't care, because the tailgate slaps and the division title feels earned in a way a Power Four ring-chaser would never understand. Outsiders call it a "weak conference" until a C-USA quarterback torches their alma mater in a bowl game and suddenly nobody's laughing.

## Current Context
Spring 2026 finds C-USA in a quiet rebuild mode after another round of roster shuffling in the transfer portal, with Liberty's full FBS footprint still casting a long shadow even in their absence. UTSA and Sam Houston are the programs everybody is watching to see if they can push into the AAC conversation or if they become the next targets on the poaching list.

## Calendar Moment
Spring practices are wrapping up and fans are circling the early non-conference schedules dropping this summer — C-USA programs live and die by those upset opportunities against Power Four opponents, and this cycle the matchups look juicy enough to actually move a needle or two.
