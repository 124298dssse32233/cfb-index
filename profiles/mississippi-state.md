## program_history

Mississippi State football's modern peak was Dan Mullen's 2014 #1 ranking — six weeks atop the AP poll, Dak Prescott as Heisman finalist, an Orange Bowl appearance. The Mike Leach era (2020-2022) layered an Air Raid offensive identity on top of the SEC-pragmatic foundation. Jeff Lebby's 2024 hire is the next bet.

## fanbase_summary

Bulldog fans are cowbell-loyalist-patient. The cowbells are the program's most-cited cultural asset; Davis Wade Stadium tailgates skew working-class Mississippi by demographic. The Egg Bowl is the calendar gravitational center; SEC competitive reality is the year-round patience requirement.

## rivalry_context

Ole Miss (Egg Bowl) is the structural rival — same state, same Thanksgiving week, opposite cultural identity. Alabama is the SEC West blocker. LSU is the regional rival the SEC East-West realignment muddled. The 2024-2025 SEC-only divisionless era is reshaping every meaningful rivalry context.
