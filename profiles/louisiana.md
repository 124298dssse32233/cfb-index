## program_history

Louisiana football's modern peak was Billy Napier's 2018-2021 4 consecutive Sun Belt championships + 2021 13-1 + #19 final AP rank (program's all-time peak). Napier's 2022 departure to Florida took the methodology with him. Michael Desormeaux's 2022-present tenure continues the cycle. The Lafayette Acadiana cultural identity + Cajun Field venue anchor permanent positioning.

## fanbase_summary

Ragin' Cajun fans are Lafayette-Cajun-Geaux-loyalists. The Acadiana French-Louisiana cultural identity + Napier 4-title dynasty + Cajun Field venue + Sun Belt conference identity combine into a fanbase distinct from the larger Louisiana flagship programs (LSU, Tulane).

## rivalry_context

Louisiana-Monroe (the Battle on the Bayou) is the In-State Sun Belt rival. App State + Coastal Carolina + Marshall + Troy are the Sun Belt cycle annual partners. The Sun Belt realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
