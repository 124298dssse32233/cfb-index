## program_history

Indiana football's modern peak is Curt Cignetti's 2024 11-1 season — the first 11-win season in program history, the first CFP appearance, the breakthrough year that ended decades of Big Ten football mediocrity. Pre-Cignetti highlights were sparse — Tom Allen's 2020 6-2 mini-peak, Bill Mallory's 1980s-90s era. The Cignetti hire from James Madison (where he won the FCS national title) is the structural reset.

## fanbase_summary

Hoosier fans are Bloomington-basketball-first-football-convert. The basketball program's five national championships (and Bobby Knight era) are the cultural anchor; football's 2024 emergence created a parallel football fanbase identity that didn't previously exist. Memorial Stadium tailgates have transformed since the 2024 11-win run.

## rivalry_context

Purdue (Old Oaken Bucket) is the structural in-state Big Ten rival — 90+ meetings, the trophy with the alternating-letters chain. Michigan (the Big Ten East rivalry by geography) is the structural blocker. Penn State (post-realignment) is the conference fixture. The 2024 CFP appearance changed Indiana's positioning across every rivalry.
