## program_history

Syracuse football's all-time peak is the 1959 11-0 national championship under Ben Schwartzwalder. Modern peak is Fran Brown's 2024 10-3 (his first year as head coach). The jersey #44 lineage — Jim Brown → Ernie Davis → Floyd Little — produced three Hall of Famers + Davis's 1961 Heisman (the first by a Black player). The 1980 Carrier Dome construction created the only P5 on-campus dome. The 2022 dome-roof replacement modernized for the next era.

## fanbase_summary

Orange fans are upstate-NY-orange-loyalists. The northeast-football identity + Carrier Dome winter-football climate + retired #44 historical anchor combine into a fanbase that values heritage equal to wins. The Fran Brown 2024 era's competitive reset brought back broader regional engagement.

## rivalry_context

Penn State (the historic non-conference rival, pre-Big-East-realignment) was the structural fixture; the post-realignment ACC cycle replaced it. Boston College (the ACC fixture, the structural Northeast rival) is the modern annual game. The Big East / ACC realignment shuffles continue to reshape rival cycles.
