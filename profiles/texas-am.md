## program_history

Texas A&M's 1939 national title is the only one. The 76-year span between that and the 2014 Johnny Manziel-era top-5 finish defines the program's relationship with patience. The 2012 SEC move was the bet that the institution's resource base would translate; the 2024 Cotton Bowl seems to have validated it. The 2025 season is the test of whether the bet compounds.

## fanbase_summary

Aggie fans are tradition-cathedral-builders. The institutional rituals (Midnight Yell, 12th Man, Aggie Ring Day) outweigh the on-field record. SEC home games at Kyle Field consistently rank as the loudest non-LSU SEC venues. The fanbase has been patient; the patience may be running out.

## rivalry_context

Texas is the historical rival the 2011 Big 12 collapse paused for a decade. The 2024 series resumption made the calendar interesting again. LSU is the SEC adopted-rivalry. Arkansas is the geographical rival the SEC has slow-burned into legitimacy.
