## program_history

Florida Atlantic football began in 2001 under Howard Schnellenberger (Miami 1983 national championship coach). The program went FCS 2001 → FBS 2005 → first bowl 2007 (New Orleans Bowl championship). The modern peak was Lane Kiffin's 2017 11-3 + Conference USA championship + Boca Raton Bowl championship + #24 final AP. The Kiffin era (2017-2019) + Willie Taggart era (2020-2022) + Tom Herman era (2023-present) continued the program's modern arc.

## fanbase_summary

Owl fans are Boca-Owl-Winning-In-Paradise. The Howard Schnellenberger founding narrative + Lane Kiffin 2017 breakthrough + 'Winning In Paradise' identity + Boca Raton beach-paradise geographic catchment + American Athletic conference identity combine into a fanbase distinct from larger Florida flagship-state programs (Florida, FSU, Miami, USF, UCF).

## rivalry_context

Florida International (the Shula Bowl) is the In-State C-USA / Sun Belt rival, with renewed competitive matchups across conference cycles. The AAC partners + UTSA + Rice are the modern annual cycle fixtures. The American Athletic realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
