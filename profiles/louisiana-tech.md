## program_history

Louisiana Tech football's notable narratives span Terry Bradshaw's 1968-1969 QB development (#1 overall NFL draft 1970, 4 Super Bowl rings with Steelers), the 1973 NCAA Division II national championship under Maxie Lambright, and Skip Holtz's 2013-2018 era (multiple bowl wins + 2014 Heart of Dallas Bowl championship). Sonny Cumbie's 2022-present tenure continues the cycle.

## fanbase_summary

Bulldog fans are Ruston-Bulldog-Dunkin-loyalists. The Bradshaw historical anchor + 1973 D-II championship + Skip Holtz era + Joe Aillet Stadium venue + Conference USA conference identity combine into a fanbase distinct from larger Louisiana flagship programs (LSU, Tulane, Louisiana).

## rivalry_context

Southern Miss (the Rivalry Trophy, since 1922) is the historic G5 rival. UTEP + WKU + the C-USA cycle partners are the modern annual fixtures. The C-USA realignment continues to develop rival cycles.
