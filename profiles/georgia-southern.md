Georgia Southern football's all-time peak was the 6 FCS national championships dynasty (1985-2000) under Erk Russell, Paul Johnson, and Mike Sewak. The Triple-Option offensive identity (Paul Johnson era) gives the program a permanent scheme legacy. The 2014 FBS reclassification produced an immediate Sun Belt championship in the first FBS season — unprecedented. Clay Helton's 2022-present tenure continues the cycle.
