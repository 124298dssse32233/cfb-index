## program_history

Virginia football's competitive peaks have been brief — George Welsh's 1989-1995 ACC competitiveness (multiple bowl wins), Al Groh's mid-2000s top-25 stretches. The 2022 shooting (three UVA football players killed in an on-campus tragedy) reshaped the program's culture in ways that transcended any season's competitive narrative. Tony Elliott's 2022-present tenure is rebuilding through that grief.

## fanbase_summary

Cavalier fans are Charlottesville-academic-patient. The Wahoowa identity is universal in alumni gatherings; the Good Old Song's hand-on-shoulder unity at game's end is uniquely UVA. Scott Stadium tailgates with the Blue Ridge backdrop are among the ACC's most-cited scenic experiences.

## rivalry_context

Virginia Tech (Commonwealth Cup) is the in-state ACC structural rivalry — Charlottesville-academic vs Blacksburg-engineering, the cultural overlay matters more than the football. Maryland (the Comm Cup's lesser predecessor, lost to realignment) was the historic ACC rival.
