ANOMALY

Penn State Closes 2025 With Four Straight, Including Clemson

The Nittany Lions ended 2025 on a four-game winning streak, beating Michigan State, Nebraska, Rutgers, and Clemson by margins of 18, 27, 4, and 12. Only one of those wins was decided by fewer than a touchdown before the fourth, and that was Rutgers — the rest weren't close. Finishing the season on a run like this is the kind of momentum Franklin's program has learned to stockpile heading into recruiting January.

Ohio State Has Won Three Straight Over the Nittany Lions

Penn State dropped a 14-38 decision to Ohio State in 2025, the third consecutive loss to the Buckeyes and the most lopsided of the run. The margins read 24, 7, and 8 — close enough twice to feel like something, wide enough in 2025 to sting differently. Franklin hasn't beaten Ohio State since 2016, and that drought is the clearest unfinished business left in Happy Valley.

ANOMALY

Penn State Wins Big at Home, Barely Survives on the Road in 2025

Through twelve games in 2025, Penn State is averaging a +18.9 margin inside Beaver Stadium and -1.6 on the road — a 20-point swing that is one of the starkest home-away splits in the country. The Nittany Lions have been a different team once they leave Happy Valley, grinding out road trips that look nothing like the comfortable wins in front of the White Out crowd. That gap is worth watching as the schedule tightens.
