THE ERA · 2014 THROUGH 2025

The CFP Era

The CFP era is the one Auburn spent on the schedule the SEC West could not reliably schedule around. A Kick Six wound, an SEC West title in 2017, a Malzahn exit, a Harsin interruption, and the Freeze rebuild. No CFP bids; two head-coach fires; one rivalry that still bends every season's shape.
