Rituals

Army-Navy Game

since 1890

The Army-Navy game — first played 1890, only paused for World Wars — is one of CFB's most-storied rivalries. The Cadets corps + Brigade of Midshipmen perform full march-on, the Commander-in-Chief watches in person, the game's pageantry is unmatched. Every senior plays their last college game here.

RSpermanent historic

Roger Staubach 1963 Heisman

since 1963

Roger Staubach's 1963 Heisman + 9-1 season + Cotton Bowl appearance + #2 final AP rank — the program's all-time peak. Staubach's post-Navy career (Cowboys + Hall of Fame) added permanent legacy. The Roger Staubach statue + retired #12 jersey + biographical canon are permanent Navy identity.

BGpermanent mascot i

Bill the Goat Mascot

since 1893

Bill the Goat — Navy's costumed mascot since 1893 — is one of CFB's oldest live-animal-mascot traditions. The goat appears at every home game + the Army-Navy game + recruiting events. The 'Anchors Aweigh' fight song + Bill's stadium walk-on are permanent visuals.
