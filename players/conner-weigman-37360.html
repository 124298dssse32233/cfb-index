Why this is a two-channel quarterback case

The fast read on the thing that makes this player more than a generic stat line.

The unusual part of Conner Weigman's case is that the production comes in two channels, not one. For Houston, he has 2,705 passing yards and 25 touchdown passes, but he also adds 703 rushing yards and 11 rushing scores. That kind of dual-threat load tends to create a more resilient Heisman case because the offense can tilt the game in multiple ways.
