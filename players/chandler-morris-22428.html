Why this is a two-channel quarterback case

The fast read on the thing that makes this player more than a generic stat line.

The unusual part of Chandler Morris's case is that the production comes in two channels, not one. For Virginia, he has 3,000 passing yards and 16 touchdown passes, but he also adds 245 rushing yards and 5 rushing scores. That kind of dual-threat load tends to create a more resilient Heisman case because the offense can tilt the game in multiple ways.
